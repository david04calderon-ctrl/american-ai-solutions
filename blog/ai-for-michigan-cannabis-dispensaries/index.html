Where AI Drives the Most Value in Michigan Cannabis Retail

Customer Loyalty & Personalized Outreach

Your POS system knows everything about your customers: what they buy, how often they visit, what price points they prefer, whether they're flower-focused or concentrate-focused, whether they prefer indica or sativa or CBD-dominant products. Most dispensaries use this data for nothing more than a generic loyalty point program. AI uses it to send personalized outreach: "Hey, the Alien OG you bought in March is back in stock from the same cultivator" or "Based on what you usually grab, you might like this new RSO we just got in." Personalized product notifications convert at 3–4x the rate of generic promotions. Repeat customer revenue increases 45%.

Inventory Optimization & Reorder Automation

Cannabis inventory is complex: products have shelf lives, regulatory tracking requirements, vendor lead times, and wildly variable demand based on potency, strain popularity, and price. Running out of your top sellers on a Friday afternoon costs you customers who drive to a competitor. Sitting on slow-moving flower that's aging past peak quality costs you margin and eventual write-downs. AI monitors inventory levels in real time, identifies reorder points based on historical velocity, and generates purchase orders for manager approval. You stop running out of popular products and stop accumulating dead inventory.

METRC Compliance Reporting

Michigan cannabis businesses are required to report all inventory movements, sales, and transfers to METRC in real time. Compliance failures result in license penalties and inspection risk. Manually entering METRC data consumes 2–4 hours per week for a single-location dispensary — and human data entry has a significant error rate. AI integrates with your POS and automates METRC entries, cross-checks for reconciliation errors, and flags discrepancies before they become compliance issues. Your compliance manager verifies instead of entering.

New Customer Onboarding

A customer walks in for the first time, overwhelmed by the menu. Your budtender walks them through the basics, they buy something mid-range, and they leave. 30% of first-time customers never come back — not because they had a bad experience, but because nobody followed up. AI sends a personalized follow-up message 48 hours after a first purchase: educational content about the product they bought, a question about their experience, and a recommendation for their next visit based on what they purchased. First-to-second-visit conversion improves 35–45%.

Flash Sale & Promotion Automation

Dispensaries run promotions constantly — daily deals, clearance sales, new product launches. Communicating these promotions to the right customers at the right time is a marketing challenge. AI identifies which customers are most likely to be interested in each promotion based on purchase history and sends targeted notifications (via SMS, where legally compliant). Response rates from targeted promotions run 3–5x higher than blanket promotions. Your marketing spend per converted customer drops. Clearance products move faster.

Michigan Cannabis Regulation Note: Michigan cannabis marketing is regulated by the Cannabis Regulatory Agency (CRA). All AI-generated customer communications must comply with CRA marketing rules — no marketing to persons under 21, no health claims, required disclaimers.
