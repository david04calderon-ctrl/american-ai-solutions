Where Michigan Orthodontic Practices Lose Cases

1. Consultations That Don't Get Followed Up Fast Enough

After a free consultation, families need time to think, talk to their spouse, check insurance, and make a decision. Most practices send one follow-up email. AI sends a 5-touch sequence over 14 days — email, text, educational content, insurance breakdown, and a final "we saved your spot" message. Each touchpoint is timed and personalized based on what was discussed at the consultation.

2. Insurance Confusion That Kills Decisions

The #1 reason ortho consultations don't convert: "I need to check on insurance." AI sends a proactive insurance summary within 24 hours of the consultation, breaking down estimated out-of-pocket costs and available payment plans. Removing the unknown removes the friction.

3. Appointment No-Shows and Dropped Treatment

Active orthodontic patients miss adjustment appointments, which extends treatment timelines and frustrates everyone. AI sends appointment reminders 48 hours and 2 hours before each visit. Patients in active treatment also receive monthly check-ins that keep engagement high through 18–24 month treatment plans.

The Automation Stack for Michigan Orthodontic Practices

  • Post-consultation follow-up sequence: Day 1/3/7/10/14 touches — email + text, each with a specific message and CTA
  • Insurance summary auto-send: Triggered within hours of consultation — estimated out-of-pocket and payment plan options
  • Appointment reminders: 48-hour and 2-hour text reminders for every active patient appointment
  • Treatment milestone messages: "You're halfway there!" check-ins at 25%, 50%, 75% of treatment plan
  • Sibling conversion campaign: When a patient starts treatment, automatically follow up with parents about sibling consultations
  • Review requests: Sent at debond (when braces come off) — peak satisfaction moment for requesting Google reviews
  • Referral requests: Post-debond "know someone who needs a beautiful smile?" campaign
