The Revenue Leaks Killing Michigan Nail Salon Profits

1. No-Shows and Last-Minute Cancellations

A no-show at a nail salon is dead chair time with zero revenue and a slot that could have gone to a paying client. AI sends appointment reminders via text 48 hours and 4 hours before every appointment — with a one-tap confirm button. No-show rates drop by 35–55%.

2. Clients Who Forget to Rebook

A client leaves happy after a full set. Three weeks later, her nails are growing out and she needs a refill — but she's busy and hasn't booked yet. AI sends a personalized "time for a refill" text at the right interval based on their service history. These messages book at 30–40%.

3. Lapsed Clients Who Switched Salons

Clients who haven't been in for 6+ weeks are at high risk of going elsewhere permanently. AI identifies them and sends a win-back message — a special offer, a "we miss you," or just a quick personal check-in. Win-back campaigns convert 15–22% of at-risk clients.

What the AI System Handles Automatically

  • Appointment reminders: Text at 48 hours and 4 hours — confirm, reschedule, or cancel link included
  • Rebooking nudges: Sent at 3 weeks for fills, 6 weeks for full sets — timed by service type
  • Win-back campaign: Triggered when a client hasn't booked in 45+ days
  • Seasonal promotions: Valentine's Day, prom season, Mother's Day, holiday — targeted to clients by service history
  • Review requests: Sent after appointment completion to satisfied clients — drives Google and Yelp visibility
  • Referral asks: After the 5th visit, automatically asks happy clients if they have friends who'd love the salon
