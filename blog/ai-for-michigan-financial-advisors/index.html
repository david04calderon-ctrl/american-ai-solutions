Where Michigan Financial Advisors Lose Clients and AUM

1. Prospects Who Go Cold After Initial Interest

A prospect comes in for a financial planning consultation. The meeting goes well. You follow up once. They say they need to think about it. You follow up again a week later. And then the ball drops. AI builds a 90-day prospect nurture sequence — educational content, market insights, retirement planning tips, and timely personal check-ins — that keeps you visible without being pushy. Prospect-to-client conversion improves 20–30%.

2. Quarterly Reviews That Never Get Scheduled

Most financial advisors know they should hold quarterly reviews with their top clients. Most don't, because scheduling 40–50 quarterly reviews requires manual outreach that competes with everything else on your plate. AI sends scheduling invites automatically at the right time, tracks who's responded, and follows up with non-responders — so every client gets their review without you managing the calendar manually.

3. Clients Who Feel Forgotten Between Meetings

Client attrition in financial advisory often happens not because of performance, but because of communication. Clients who don't hear from their advisor during market volatility assume they're not paying attention. AI sends market commentary, relevant financial planning reminders (tax deadlines, RMD windows, open enrollment), and personal check-ins that make clients feel watched over — without requiring the advisor to manually draft each message.

What the AI System Manages (Compliance-Aware)

  • Prospect nurture sequence: 90-day educational and check-in sequence for warm prospects
  • Quarterly review scheduling: Automated outreach 3 weeks before each client's review window
  • Market volatility communications: Pre-approved message templates sent during market moves
  • Financial planning reminders: RMD deadlines, tax loss harvesting window, open enrollment, Q4 charitable giving
  • Life event follow-up: Retirement, marriage, inheritance, job change — triggers a personalized planning check-in
  • Referral outreach: Annual "do you know someone who could benefit from financial planning?" to top clients
Compliance Note: All AI-generated client communications are templated and reviewed by the advisor before deployment. We build the system around your compliance framework — whether you're RIA, broker-dealer, or dually registered. We do not generate specific investment advice in automated messages.
