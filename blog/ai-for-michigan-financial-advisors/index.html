SEC & FINRA Compliance: What Michigan Advisors Must Know About AI

Regulatory Framework for AI in Michigan Financial Advisory Practices

SEC Marketing Rule (Rule 206(4)-1): AI-generated client communications and marketing materials must be reviewed by a supervised registered representative before distribution. AI can draft; a human advisor must review and approve. The advisor remains responsible for every communication sent under their name.

FINRA Rule 4511 (Electronic Recordkeeping): All client communications, including AI-generated emails reviewed and sent by advisors, must be retained in compliant electronic recordkeeping systems for 6 years. Our AI implementations integrate with FINRA-approved email archiving systems automatically.

AI-Generated Investment Recommendations: AI in our implementations does not generate investment recommendations or provide investment advice — it prepares information for advisor review. The advisor makes all recommendations. This preserves fiduciary duty and regulatory compliance.

Michigan DIFS Oversight: Michigan investment advisers registered with the Department of Insurance and Financial Services follow Michigan Uniform Securities Act requirements. AI implementations we build for Michigan RIAs are designed to support, not circumvent, these requirements.
