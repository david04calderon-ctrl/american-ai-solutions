Where Michigan Optometry Practices Lose Revenue

1. No-Shows on Scheduled Exams

An optometry no-show is an exam slot that can't be filled last-minute — and with dilation and testing time, each slot is 45–60 minutes of revenue. AI sends appointment reminders via text 48 hours and 4 hours before every scheduled exam, cutting no-show rates by 35–50%.

2. Annual Recall Patients Who Never Get Contacted

Most practices have a recall list of patients who are due or overdue for their annual exam. The problem: someone has to pull the list and send the messages. AI does this automatically — every patient who hasn't been in for 11 months gets a "time for your annual eye exam" message in month 11, a reminder in month 12, and a final outreach in month 14.

3. Contact Lens Reorders That Go Elsewhere

A patient's contact lens supply runs out. Instead of ordering through your practice, they buy from 1-800-CONTACTS or Costco. AI sends a contact lens reorder reminder 60 days before their supply runs out — with your practice's ordering link. Reorder capture rates improve 25–40%.

What the AI System Manages

  • Appointment reminders: 48-hour and 4-hour text reminders with confirm/reschedule link
  • Annual recall sequence: Month 11, 12, and 14 personalized recall messages for every patient
  • Contact lens reorder reminders: Triggered 60 days before estimated supply depletion
  • Eyewear purchase follow-up: 30-day check-in after new glasses or contacts — "how are they working?"
  • Pediatric exam reminders: Back-to-school campaign (July/August) for families with children
  • Insurance benefit reminders: "Your vision benefits reset January 1 — book before they expire" campaigns
  • Review requests: Sent to satisfied patients after positive exams
Michigan Insurance Angle: Michigan has strong employer vision benefits through Blue Cross Blue Shield, Priority Health, Delta Dental Vision, and VSP — but many patients don't use them before they reset. A December "use it before you lose it" campaign targeting patients with unused benefits is one of the highest-converting campaigns in optometry. AI runs it automatically every year.
