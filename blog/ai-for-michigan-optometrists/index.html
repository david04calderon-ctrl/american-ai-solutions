Michigan has over 1,200 licensed optometrists — serving a population that increasingly relies on corrective lenses, contact lenses, and annual eye health monitoring. Where Michigan Optometry Practices Lose Revenue

Patient Recall Failures

The standard of care for most patients is an annual comprehensive eye exam. But if your recall system is a postcard mailed once and a front desk that manually calls overdue patients, you're losing 20–25% of your active patient base to attrition every year. AI runs a systematic multi-touchpoint recall sequence: email at 11 months, SMS at 12 months, follow-up at 13 months, final reactivation at 15 months — with direct scheduling links at every touchpoint. Patients who would have drifted to VSP/Davis Vision providers rebook with you.

Insurance Verification Delays

Manual insurance verification at the front desk takes 5–10 minutes per patient — verifying vision benefits, co-pay amounts, frame allowances, and contact lens coverage before the appointment. For a busy practice seeing 25–35 patients per day, that's 2–4 hours of staff time daily. AI verifies insurance benefits automatically 48–72 hours before each appointment and delivers a benefits summary to the patient — eliminating the wait at check-in and the manual staff burden.

Contact Lens Reorder Gaps

Patients who wear daily, bi-weekly, or monthly contacts run out on predictable schedules. Without an automated reorder reminder, they order from 1-800 Contacts or a competing provider's online store rather than your practice — losing you both the contact lens revenue and the annual exam visit that comes with an Rx renewal. AI sends reorder prompts at the right interval based on lens type and box count purchased, with a direct link to your online optical store or order form.

Eyeglass Purchase Conversion

After a comprehensive exam, many patients with a new Rx take their prescription and go elsewhere to buy frames — to Warby Parker, Costco, or an online retailer. Without a systematic engagement sequence, you lose the optical sale. AI sends a "your new Rx is ready" notification with a link to your frame selection, a virtual try-on offer (if applicable), and a limited-time new patient eyeglass offer — capturing optical revenue from patients who had already decided to walk out.

HIPAA Note: All patient communication and insurance verification systems are built to HIPAA standards — PHI-compliant messaging, BAA in place, no patient data used for AI training. Michigan optometry practices require strict data handling; every system we build meets the standard before go-live.

What AI Automates for Michigan Optometry Practices

Annual Recall Sequence

Every patient's last exam date triggers an automatic recall sequence 11 months later: email with scheduling link, SMS at 12 months, follow-up email at 13 months with urgency language around vision benefit expiration, final reactivation at 15 months with a "we miss you" message. Patients who book at any touchpoint are removed from the sequence. Your schedule fills with returning patients without front desk effort.

Automated Insurance Pre-Verification

48 hours before each appointment, AI verifies vision insurance benefits for the scheduled patient — coverage type, co-pay, frame allowance, contact lens benefit — and sends a pre-visit summary to the patient. Check-in takes 60 seconds. Your staff focuses on patient experience, not insurance phone queues.

Contact Lens Reorder Campaigns

Based on lens type and quantity purchased, AI calculates each patient's expected supply depletion date and sends a reorder reminder 2–3 weeks before they run out — with a direct order link or scheduling prompt for an Rx renewal if they're due. Contact lens revenue stays in your practice.

Post-Exam Eyeglass Engagement

Same-day as the exam: "Your new prescription is ready" message with frame viewing invitation and current optical promotions. Day 3: follow-up with testimonials from patients who upgraded frames. Day 7: final offer with a frame discount before the promotion expires. Optical conversion rates from same-day exam patients increase significantly with systematic engagement.

New Patient Onboarding

New patients receive a pre-visit packet: what to expect, how to find the office, what to bring (insurance cards, current glasses), and intake forms to complete before arrival. Your doctor starts each new patient exam with complete intake information — not 10 minutes of paperwork at the chair.
