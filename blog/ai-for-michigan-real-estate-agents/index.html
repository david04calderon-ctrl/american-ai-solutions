Six AI Workflows Michigan Agents Are Deploying

Lead Response & Nurture Automation

New lead from Zillow, Realtor.com, or your website triggers personalized response in under 60 seconds — 24/7. Michigan-specific: different scripts for Metro Detroit suburban buyers vs. U.P. land buyers vs. lake property seekers. 90-day nurture sequence runs automatically until they're ready.

Listing Description Writing

Agent inputs property facts and photos. AI writes compelling MLS description, social media captions, email marketing copy, and neighborhood narrative. Consistent quality on every listing — not just the ones you had time to write well this week.

Transaction Coordination

From accepted offer to close, AI tracks every deadline: inspection period, appraisal, mortgage commitment, closing date. Sends automated reminders to all parties. Flags delayed items before they blow the timeline. You manage the relationship; AI manages the checklist.

Past Client Re-Engagement

AI tracks every past client's home purchase anniversary, sends market update reports for their neighborhood, and surfaces re-engagement opportunities (equity has grown 30%, their area is seeing bidding wars again). Systematic relationship maintenance that generates referrals and repeat business.

Showing Feedback & Offer Prep

Post-showing feedback requests sent automatically to buyer's agents. Responses aggregated into seller-ready reports. When offers are imminent, AI prepares comparison summaries for sellers — net proceeds, contingencies, timeline — so you walk in ready to advise, not still running numbers.

Social Media & Market Report Automation

Weekly Michigan market stats (days on market, list-to-sale ratio, median price by zip) compiled and formatted for social media and email lists automatically. You review and post — no starting from scratch every week. Consistency builds your local authority.

    Sub-60-Second First Response

    Lead submits web form at 10pm on a Friday. AI sends personalized response within 45 seconds: acknowledges their specific search criteria, includes 2–3 matching active listings, invites them to schedule a call. Your competitors respond Monday morning. You responded in under a minute.

    Intelligent Lead Qualification

    Michigan-Specific Content Drip

    Nurture sequence customized by buyer profile: first-time buyer in Macomb County gets first-time buyer program info (MSHDA Down Payment Assistance, federal tax credit eligibility). Downsizer from Bloomfield Hills gets empty-nester neighborhood guides. Lake buyer gets seasonal property ownership content.

    Market Activity Triggers

    When a property matching the lead's saved search drops in price, goes under contract (creating urgency), or a new match hits the market, AI sends personalized alert. The lead stays engaged with your brand because you're the one always bringing them relevant intelligence.

    Warm Hand-Off to Agent

    When engagement signals spike — multiple listing views, price drop response, direct question — AI flags the lead to you with a context brief: what they've clicked, how long they've been searching, what they said they wanted. You call already knowing everything. Conversation converts.

Michigan Real Estate License Law & AI: What You Must Know

Michigan's Real Estate License Law (MCL 339.2501 et seq.) and NAR Code of Ethics apply to all agent communications — including AI-generated ones. Key compliance requirements: any AI-generated communication must be reviewed before sending if it contains specific property claims or price representations; the agent of record remains responsible for all communications sent under their license; and AI-generated listing descriptions must be factually accurate and not contain material misrepresentations. Automated disclosures (agency disclosure, lead disclosure) must still be delivered in the required format and timing — AI doesn't create exemptions from disclosure law.
