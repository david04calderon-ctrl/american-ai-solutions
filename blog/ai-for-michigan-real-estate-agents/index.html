What AI Automates for Michigan Real Estate Agents

1. Lead Intake and Instant Nurture

When a lead comes in from Zillow, Realtor.com, your website, or a referral, AI captures their information, sends an instant personalized response within 60 seconds, and starts a multi-touch nurture sequence. AI qualifies the lead (buyer/seller, timeline, price range, preapproval status) and routes hot leads to your phone immediately. Cold leads get nurtured automatically for months until they're ready.

2. MLS Listing Description Generation

Give AI the property specs, key features, and neighborhood highlights. It generates a polished, SEO-optimized MLS description in under 5 minutes — tailored to your brand voice. Social media captions, email announcements, and property website copy all come out in the same workflow. What used to take 45 minutes of staring at a blank screen takes 5 minutes of review.

3. Showing Coordination and Scheduling

AI connects to your calendar and showing platforms (ShowingTime, Calendly) and handles scheduling requests automatically. When a buyer's agent requests a showing, AI checks availability, confirms with all parties, sends reminders 24 hours and 2 hours before, and logs the appointment to your CRM — no phone tag, no back-and-forth email chains.

4. Transaction Timeline Management

After a purchase agreement is signed, AI monitors critical deadlines — inspection period, financing contingency, appraisal window, closing date — and sends automated reminders to all parties (buyer, seller, lender, title company) as deadlines approach. Nothing falls through the cracks because someone forgot to follow up.

5. Client Update Automation

Buyers and sellers want to know what's happening — constantly. AI sends automated status updates at key milestones: offer accepted, inspection scheduled, appraisal ordered, clear to close, closing confirmed. Clients feel informed. You stop answering the same "what's the status?" text 12 times a day.
