Where Michigan Interior Design Studios Lose Revenue

1. Consultation Inquiries That Go Quiet

Someone fills out your contact form after seeing your Instagram or getting a referral. You respond within a day, have a great initial conversation — and then life gets busy and the follow-up falls through. Two weeks later they've hired someone else.

AI builds a structured follow-up sequence: Day 1 confirmation, Day 3 portfolio send, Day 7 "any questions?", Day 14 case study with ROI. Lead-to-consultation conversion typically improves 25–35% with systematic nurture.

2. Past Clients Who Never Get Touched Again

Your best leads are people who've already hired you. They trust you, they know your work, and they may have a second project — or a friend who needs one. Most interior designers have 50–200 past clients they never contact between projects.

AI builds a past client reactivation sequence: annual anniversary of the project completion, seasonal "thinking of refreshing a room?" messages, and personal check-ins. These convert at 8–15% — and each converted past client is worth $8,000–$25,000+ in project revenue.

3. Referral Requests That Never Get Made

Interior design lives on word-of-mouth. But most designers are too busy (or too uncomfortable) to systematically ask for referrals. AI sends a referral request at the peak satisfaction moment — typically at project completion or 30 days post-handoff. When the ask is well-timed and warm, response rates are 20–30%.

What AI Manages for Michigan Interior Designers

  • Inquiry follow-up sequence: 5-touch nurture over 14 days — portfolio, case studies, FAQ, availability check
  • Post-consultation follow-up: Proposal reminder at Day 3 and Day 7 if no response
  • Active project communication: Weekly status update reminders, milestone notification templates
  • Project completion sequence: Post-handoff survey, review request, referral ask — timed 3 days after completion
  • Past client reactivation: Annual anniversary message + seasonal refresh campaigns
  • Portfolio showcase: When you complete a project in a specific style, automatically share with past clients who expressed similar taste
