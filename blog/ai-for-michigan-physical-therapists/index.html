Where AI Delivers the Fastest ROI in Physical Therapy

Prior Authorization Automation

Prior auth is the single biggest administrative time sink in most PT clinics. Getting authorization for a 12-visit episode involves verifying benefits, submitting the request, following up when the payer doesn't respond, getting peer-to-peer reviews scheduled when auth is denied, and documenting everything for compliance. AI handles the initial verification and submission automatically, monitors pending requests, sends escalation alerts when timelines are exceeded, and drafts peer-to-peer request letters from clinical documentation. Your front desk staff handles exceptions — not the entire process.

Clinics implementing auth automation report recovering 6–9 hours per week per biller and reducing auth denial rates by 22% through more complete initial submissions.

Appointment Reminders & No-Show Recovery

A missed PT visit is a total loss — unlike a restaurant, you can't resell that slot last-minute. Michigan PT clinics average 14–18% no-show rates without active reminder systems. AI sends a text reminder 48 hours before, another at 24 hours, and a final reminder 2 hours before. If the patient cancels, the slot immediately goes to a waitlist with automatic fill offers. No-show rates drop to 6–8%. At 30 visits per day, that's 2–3 additional visits per day that were previously lost.

Home Exercise Program (HEP) Delivery & Adherence Tracking

Your PT spends 10 minutes after every initial eval explaining home exercises. Half of patients don't do them consistently. Poor HEP adherence is the primary driver of poor outcomes — and poor outcomes drive down your patient satisfaction scores, referral rates, and payer quality metrics. AI delivers personalized HEP instructions via text or email immediately after each session, sends daily adherence check-ins, and flags patients with low compliance for PT follow-up. Adherence rates improve from 58% to 92% with automated HEP systems. Better adherence = better outcomes = more referrals = more revenue.

Insurance Verification at Intake

How many times has a patient arrived for their first visit and your front desk discovers their insurance lapsed, or that PT requires a referral from their PCP that they don't have? AI runs insurance verification automatically when a new patient books, checks for referral requirements, flags missing documentation, and contacts the patient or their physician before the appointment. You eliminate surprise coverage denials and the difficult conversation that follows.

Billing Follow-Up & Denial Management

The average Michigan PT clinic has $18,000–$32,000 in aged claims sitting in the 60–120+ day bucket. Most of these aren't uncollectable — they're just under-chased. AI monitors aging AR, generates follow-up requests at 30/60/90 day intervals, drafts denial appeal letters from clinical notes, and escalates complex cases to your biller. Clinics report recovering $2,500–$3,500 per month in previously written-off claims within the first 90 days of implementation.

Outcomes That Compound Over Time

The immediate ROI from scheduling and billing automation is measurable within 30 days. But the longer-term value compounds in ways that are harder to quantify but more impactful:
