Michigan HVAC Revenue Stability Starts With Maintenance Agreements

An HVAC company with 100 maintenance agreement clients has $320,000 in guaranteed annual revenue before a single emergency call or system replacement. With 300 agreements it is $960,000. Maintenance agreements are the highest-leverage asset in an HVAC business because they guarantee revenue, guarantee repeat contact, and create first-right-of-refusal on every system replacement. AI sells and renews them automatically.

A Michigan HVAC company that grows from 50 to 150 maintenance agreement clients adds $320,000 in guaranteed annual recurring revenue — plus the system replacement priority that converts 40% of agreement clients into $5,800+ replacement jobs when their system fails.

Maintenance Agreement Sales Campaigns

After every service call and system installation, AI sends a maintenance agreement offer: "Now that your system is serviced, want to protect it with an annual agreement? We include spring AC tune-up, fall furnace tune-up, priority scheduling, and a 15% discount on repairs. Michigan winters are not the time to find out your furnace has a problem." Agreement conversion after service runs 35 to 50%.

Seasonal Tune-Up Campaigns

AI sends two major campaign sequences per year: Spring AC Tune-Up (April) and Fall Furnace Tune-Up (September). "Michigan summer is 6 weeks away and our schedule is filling fast. Book your AC tune-up now and avoid the rush. Catching small problems now prevents $2,000 emergency calls in July." Seasonal campaign pre-books reduce peak-season scheduling chaos and capture customers before they call a competitor.

Emergency Response Lead Capture

Michigan HVAC emergencies happen at night, on weekends, and on the coldest days of the year. AI captures every after-hours emergency inquiry immediately: "We received your emergency HVAC request. Our technician will call you back within [timeframe]. For a no-heat emergency in freezing temperatures, call [emergency line] now." Immediate response on emergency requests captures 70%+ of callers who would go to the next available company.

System Age Replacement Campaigns

HVAC systems last 12 to 18 years. AI tracks installation dates and sends replacement campaigns at year 12: "Your system is approaching the end of its typical lifespan. Michigan winters are hard on older systems. Michigan Energy rebates of $300 to $1,500 are available for qualifying high-efficiency replacements this year. Want a free assessment before it fails at the worst time?" Replacement campaigns convert at 30 to 45% because the homeowner has been watching the system age.

Commercial Account Development

Michigan commercial accounts — offices, restaurants, retail, industrial — need preventive maintenance contracts that residential customers do not. AI sends targeted commercial outreach to local businesses: "We service residential HVAC throughout [area] and are expanding our commercial preventive maintenance program. Rooftop units, commercial split systems, and boiler systems. Want a free commercial assessment?" Commercial maintenance agreements average $4,500 to $12,000 per year and renew automatically.

Indoor Air Quality Upsells

AI adds an indoor air quality upsell sequence to every service call and tune-up completion: "Michigan homes are sealed tight in winter — indoor air quality is often worse than outdoor. UV air purifiers, whole-home humidifiers, and HEPA filtration systems improve air quality and protect your HVAC investment. Want to see the options?" IAQ upsells average $800 to $2,400 and convert at 25 to 35% when presented after a successful service experience.
