Where AI Delivers the Most Value in Catering

Instant Quote Generation

A couple inquires about wedding catering for 180 guests on a Saturday in September. In a manual system, you schedule a consultation, spend an hour in conversation, and deliver a proposal 3–5 days later. In an AI system, you collect the key parameters (guest count, meal type, service style, dietary requirements, venue location) via a brief intake form and generate a detailed proposal within hours. You're presenting options while competitors are still scheduling their consultation. Catering operators using AI-assisted quoting report booking 35% more events at the same staff level.

Dietary Restriction & Menu Management

Managing dietary restrictions for a large event is a liability issue, not just a preference. A guest with a severe nut allergy who receives the wrong plate is a medical emergency and a lawsuit. AI collects dietary information from guests via automated pre-event communication, generates kitchen production sheets with clear labeling for each dietary requirement, and creates server briefing documents that specify which guests have which restrictions. Nothing falls through the cracks because nothing is being tracked manually in someone's memory.

Event Timeline & Staff Coordination

Every catering event requires a production timeline: when food prep starts, when staff arrive, load-in and setup timing, service schedule, breakdown and cleanup. For a busy weekend with 3 simultaneous events, this coordination is a full-time job. AI generates event timelines from your standard templates with event-specific customization, assigns staff to each event based on availability and skill, and sends briefing documents to each team member the day before. Your event manager reviews and approves instead of building from scratch.

Deposit Follow-Up & Contract Management

Chasing deposits is uncomfortable and time-consuming. A signed contract without a deposit holds a date you might otherwise book. AI sends deposit reminders automatically at 48-hour and 7-day intervals after proposal acceptance, generates digital contracts from your standard templates, and tracks signature and payment status across all active bookings. You see which events are fully contracted and which need follow-up at a glance.

Post-Event Review Requests & Referrals

Catering referrals are gold. A happy mother of the bride tells every friend who's planning a wedding. AI sends a review request 24 hours after the event while the experience is fresh, then a referral request 7 days later when the photos are starting to arrive and the warmth of the event is still there. Referral-generated bookings cost zero in marketing spend and close at near-100% rates.
