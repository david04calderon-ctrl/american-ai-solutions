What AI Does for Michigan Medical Spas

24/7 Booking with Intelligent Scheduling

When a prospective client discovers your med spa at 11pm on Instagram, they want to book now. AI accepts bookings around the clock, asks smart questions about treatment interest, skin type, and prior treatments, and slots them with the appropriate provider. New client consultations, follow-up treatment appointments, and package redemptions all handled without front desk involvement.

Pre-Consultation Intake and Education

Before a new client arrives, AI collects intake information — medical history relevant to treatments, current medications and supplements, allergies, skincare routine, treatment history — and sends personalized educational content about the treatments they are interested in. Clients arrive informed. Consultations are more productive. Providers spend less time explaining basics and more time on individualized recommendations.

Automated Appointment Reminders and Cancellation Recovery

Three-touch reminder sequences — 72 hours, 24 hours, 2 hours before appointment — drop no-show rates from 24% to under 5%. When a cancellation occurs, AI immediately contacts the waitlist and fills the slot automatically. The provider chair stays productive. You stop losing thousands of dollars daily to empty slots.

Post-Treatment Follow-Up and Care Instructions

After every treatment, clients receive personalized aftercare instructions specific to their procedure, a check-in at 3 days to address any concerns, and a rebooking prompt at the optimal interval for their treatment type (Botox every 3-4 months, fillers every 9-12 months, laser series on a defined cadence). Clients who receive consistent follow-up rebook at 3x the rate of those who do not.

Lapsed Client Reactivation

A client database with 500 inactive clients represents significant untapped revenue. AI identifies clients who have not booked in 90+ days and sends personalized reactivation campaigns — referencing their last treatment, offering a relevant seasonal service, and providing a gentle reason to come back. A 15% reactivation rate on 500 lapsed clients is 75 appointments — $21,000 in revenue from a list that was gathering dust.

Package and Membership Upsell

Treatment packages and monthly membership programs are the highest-margin revenue in med spa. AI identifies clients who have had 2+ single-session services and presents them a personalized package offer — based on their treatment history, automatically calculated savings versus per-treatment pricing, and an easy enrollment path. Membership conversion moves from incidental to systematic.

Review Generation and Reputation Management

Med spa clients who love their results are excellent reviewers — if you ask them at the right moment. AI sends a review request 48 hours post-treatment when results are visible and satisfaction is highest. Negative signals (a concern raised in the follow-up check-in) get routed to your team for personal outreach before they become a public review. Your Google and RealSelf ratings climb. New client acquisition costs drop.

HIPAA Compliance for Medical Aesthetics

Michigan medical spas operate under both medical and cosmetic regulatory frameworks. Intake forms, medical histories, and treatment records involve protected health information. Every AI system we build for med spa clients operates on HIPAA-compliant infrastructure with BAA documentation, encrypted data handling, and audit logging. Your compliance posture improves, not worsens, when you implement AI correctly.
