Where Michigan Event Planners Lose Bookings

1. Inquiries That Go Unanswered During Busy Planning Periods

Event planning has feast-or-famine dynamics. When you're deep in a major event, inquiries for future events get delayed. AI responds within minutes of any inquiry — "We're so excited to hear about your event! Here's a little about what we do and some availability for a consultation call" — so no lead goes cold while you're head-down in execution mode.

2. Proposals That Don't Convert Without Follow-Up

You spend hours on a custom proposal, send it, and then wait. The client says they're comparing a few options. AI sends a follow-up at Day 3 ("Did you have any questions about the proposal?"), Day 7 ("We wanted to check in — the date you mentioned is in high demand"), and Day 14 ("Last check-in before we open this date up to the waitlist"). Proposal conversion rates improve 25–35%.

3. Past Clients Who Don't Send Referrals

Event planning runs on word-of-mouth. A couple you planned a beautiful wedding for will tell their friends — if they think to. AI sends a referral request 6 weeks post-event when the photos are in, the memories are vivid, and the couple is at peak satisfaction. It includes a personalized note and a referral incentive. These campaigns generate 1–3 referrals per client.

What the AI System Manages

  • Instant inquiry response: Within 2 minutes of any contact form submission — welcome + consultation scheduling link
  • Consultation confirmation sequence: Reminder + pre-call questionnaire + what to expect
  • Proposal follow-up: Day 3/7/14 sequence with personalized messaging and urgency cues
  • Active client milestone reminders: Venue deposit due, catering confirmation, guest count deadline — all automated
  • Post-event satisfaction survey: Sent 1 week after the event
  • Referral campaign: Sent 6 weeks post-event with incentive for warm referrals
  • Annual follow-up: "Your event anniversary is coming up — do you have anything coming up we can help with?"
Michigan Event Calendar: Michigan's event season peaks May–October for weddings and outdoor events, November–January for corporate holiday events, and February–March for charity galas. AI lets you run targeted campaigns to your inquiry list timed to each season — reaching people when they're actively planning and your message is most relevant.
