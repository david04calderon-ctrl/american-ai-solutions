Where Michigan Event Planners Lose Time and Revenue

Inquiry Response and Qualification

Prospect inquiries arrive at all hours — via Instagram, website forms, The Knot, and wedding show follow-ups. Each requires a personalized response, qualification conversation, and timeline-fit assessment. Without a systematic intake, you're spending 30 minutes qualifying inquiries that aren't a fit, while serious prospects who sent a Friday evening inquiry don't hear from you until Monday. AI handles first-response qualification: collecting event date, type, guest count, budget range, and vision — routing qualified leads to your discovery call calendar automatically.

Vendor Follow-Up and Confirmation

Every event involves 8–15 vendors: venue, caterer, photographer, florist, DJ or band, transportation, hair and makeup, officiant. Confirming contracts, collecting certificates of insurance, chasing final headcounts, and coordinating arrival times with each vendor is hours of administrative work per event. AI tracks every vendor deliverable per event with due dates and automatic follow-up sequences — escalating to you only when a vendor is unresponsive after 3 attempts.

Client Communication Between Milestones

Between the planning milestones — venue booking, vendor confirmation, tasting, rehearsal, event day — clients are anxious and want reassurance that everything is on track. Without proactive communication, they email with "just checking in" questions that consume your time. AI sends a weekly status update per client: what's complete, what's in progress, what's coming up next, and any action items for the client. Anxiety converts to confidence. Referrals come more easily when clients feel managed.

Post-Event Follow-Up and Referral Capture

Two weeks after a perfect event, the couple or corporate client is at peak satisfaction. This is the highest-leverage moment for a review request and referral ask. Without systematic follow-up, the moment passes and they move on. AI sends a post-event sequence: a thank-you with highlight memories, a review request, and a referral ask — capturing testimonials and word-of-mouth at the exact right moment.

What AI Automates for Michigan Event Planners

Inquiry Intake and Discovery Call Scheduling

Inquiries from any source trigger an AI intake form: event date, type, guest count, venue status, budget range, and vision description. Qualified leads are offered self-scheduling for a discovery call on your calendar. Unqualified leads receive a graceful redirect. You spend discovery calls with clients who are already informed and ready to invest — not cold contacts who've given no information.

Vendor Management Dashboard

Every event has a vendor checklist with assigned vendors, deliverable due dates, and contract status. AI sends automated follow-up reminders to vendors at 30, 14, and 7 days before deadlines. Certificates of insurance, final menus, and arrival schedules are tracked per event. Your dashboard shows exactly where each event stands — what's confirmed, what's pending, what needs your attention.

Weekly Client Status Reports

Every Friday, each active client receives an automated status email: completed this week, in progress, coming up in the next 30 days, and any action items. Clients feel informed and managed. The "just checking in" emails stop. Your inbox stays manageable even when you're managing 8 events simultaneously.

Timeline and Day-Of Coordination Documents

AI generates draft event-day timelines, vendor call sheets, and seating chart coordination documents from your event details — reducing your document prep time by 60%. You review and customize; AI builds the foundation from the information you've already collected throughout the planning process.

Review and Referral Sequence

2 weeks post-event: heartfelt thank-you with a highlight from the day. Day 21: review request with direct links to Google, The Knot, and WeddingWire. Day 45: referral ask with a simple "know anyone planning an event?" Day 90: annual anniversary or event anniversary message. Each touch maintains the relationship for future events and referrals.
