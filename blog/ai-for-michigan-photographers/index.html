What AI Automates for Michigan Photographers

1. Instant Inquiry Response and Booking Qualification

When a prospective client fills out your contact form or DMs you, AI sends a personalized reply within 60 seconds. It asks the qualifying questions (date, venue, style, budget) and routes the lead to your calendar for a consultation call — or sends your pricing guide and portfolio link based on their answers. You wake up to warm leads ready to book, not cold inquiries waiting for a reply.

2. Contract and Invoice Automation

Once a client decides to book, AI generates and sends the contract (via HoneyBook, Dubsado, or your preferred platform) with the correct package, pricing, and date. After the client signs, the deposit invoice goes out automatically. Payment reminders send at 3 days and 7 days before the due date. No more chasing. No more forgotten invoices.

3. Pre-Session Workflow

Two weeks before the session, AI sends a preparation guide personalized to the session type (wedding, portrait, newborn, commercial). One week out: location details and parking instructions. 48 hours out: weather-based reminder and what-to-wear reinforcement. Day-of: arrival time and first shot schedule. Clients show up prepared. Sessions run smooth. You shoot better work.

4. Gallery Delivery and Download Reminders

When your gallery is ready, AI sends the delivery email with the gallery link, download instructions, and a 30-day countdown. At 21 days: "Your gallery downloads in 9 days — here's your link." At 28 days: final download reminder with print product upsell. Clients download before the deadline. Print sales go up 25%.

5. Review Requests and Referral Campaigns

Seven days after gallery delivery, AI sends a review request to Google and The Knot (for wedding photographers). Two months later: a referral ask ("Know anyone getting married this year?"). At the one-year anniversary: a touchpoint message. Your past clients become your marketing department — automatically.
