What AI Does for Michigan Dental Specialty Practices

Prior Authorization Preparation and Submission

AI generates prior authorization requests from your clinical documentation — pulling the relevant diagnostic codes, radiographic findings, medical necessity language, and supporting documentation required by each Michigan payer. Submissions that previously required a dedicated administrative staff member 30–45 minutes each are prepared in under 5 minutes. Initial approval rates climb 48%. When denials occur, AI drafts the appeal using the specific clinical language and supporting evidence pattern that has historically succeeded with that payer.

Orthodontic Insurance Claims Automation

Orthodontic claims require detailed treatment narratives, diagnostic records, and phased claim submissions across 12–24 months of treatment. AI manages the claim lifecycle — initial submission with complete diagnostic narrative, phase completion claims, and treatment progress documentation — automatically from your clinical notes. Unpaid phase claims that fall through the cracks are flagged and resubmitted without manual monitoring. Orthodontic revenue per completed case improves because nothing gets missed.

Referral Network Management

Specialty dental practice is a referral-dependent business. General dentist referrals, physician referrals (for oral surgery and periodontal disease with systemic health connections), and patient self-referrals all require different management approaches. AI tracks every referral source, maintains communication with referring providers, alerts you when a referral source has dropped off, and manages the thank-you communication and case update workflow that keeps referring dentists sending patients to your practice rather than a competitor.

Treatment Plan Presentation and Financial Coordination

Complex dental specialty treatment plans — full arch implant cases, comprehensive orthodontic treatment, surgical periodontal therapy — require detailed financial presentations that explain insurance coverage, out-of-pocket costs, and payment plan options clearly. AI generates treatment plan financial summaries tailored to each patient's specific insurance coverage, presenting the information in a format that reduces sticker shock and improves case acceptance. Treatment acceptance rates for high-value cases improve 28% with AI-generated financial presentations.
