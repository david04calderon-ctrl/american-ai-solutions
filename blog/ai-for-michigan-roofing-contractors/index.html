What AI Automates for Michigan Roofing Contractors

1. Instant Lead Response — Day or Night, Storm or No Storm

When a lead comes in at 11 PM after a hailstorm — via Google, your website, Angi, or a text — AI responds within 60 seconds: professional acknowledgment, storm damage intake questions (date of storm, nature of damage, address, insurance carrier), and inspection scheduling. The customer feels attended to immediately. You capture the lead before they call the next company on Google's list.

2. Insurance Claim Navigation Support

Michigan homeowners filing roofing insurance claims often don't know the process. AI sends an automated insurance claim guidance sequence: how to document damage, what to expect from an adjuster visit, how your company coordinates with their insurance carrier, and what to look for in a contractor. Customers who feel guided and informed don't shop around. They sign with the company that helped them understand the process.

3. Estimate Follow-Up Sequences

Roofing estimates are high-dollar decisions — homeowners think, compare, and sometimes go silent for weeks. AI runs a 7-touch follow-up sequence after every estimate: value reinforcement ("Our crews are GAF-certified Michigan residents — here's why that matters"), insurance timeline prompts ("Has your adjuster scheduled a visit yet? We can coordinate."), urgency updates ("We have an opening next week before our schedule fills"), and a final close attempt at 30 days. Estimate close rates improve 35–50% with systematic follow-up.

4. Storm Campaign Activation

After a major storm event in your service area, AI triggers a targeted campaign to your customer base in the affected neighborhoods: "If your home was in [zip code] during last night's storm, we're offering free damage inspections this week — book now before demand peaks." Customers in affected areas who haven't called you yet get reached proactively. You become the trusted local resource, not just another company on the list.

5. Neighborhood Referral and Adjacent Property Campaigns

When you replace a roof in a neighborhood, AI sends door-hanger-equivalent digital outreach to adjacent properties: "We just replaced your neighbor's roof — same storm may have affected your home. Contractors who understand Michigan's specific claim documentation requirements, supplement process, and Xactimate pricing get paid more per job and have fewer disputes with adjusters. AI documentation workflows that generate insurance-format damage reports from inspection photos save hours per claim.
