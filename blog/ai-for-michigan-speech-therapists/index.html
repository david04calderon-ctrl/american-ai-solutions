What AI Does for Michigan Speech Therapists

Session Note Generation

After each session, AI generates a draft SOAP note or progress note based on your session template and clinical observations. You review, add clinical detail, and approve — a process that takes 5-8 minutes instead of 20-25. Over 30 patient sessions per week, that is 7-9 hours returned to you every week. In a year, that is 350+ hours of clinical capacity recovered.

Notes are structured to support insurance requirements — measurable goals, objective data, functional outcomes language — reducing the documentation-related denials that cost Michigan SLPs significant revenue annually.

Insurance Authorization Management

Michigan insurance authorization for speech therapy is one of the most time-consuming administrative tasks in SLP practice. AI tracks authorization status for every patient, generates re-authorization letters with the clinical data insurers require, flags authorizations expiring within 30 days, and manages the documentation trail needed for appeals when authorizations are initially denied.

Patient Scheduling and Waitlist Management

Michigan has a shortage of speech therapists, particularly for pediatric patients. Most SLP practices in Michigan have waitlists. AI manages waitlist communication — regular updates to families, priority notifications when slots open, and intake coordination so new patients arrive with all required documentation completed before their first session. Families on your waitlist do not go silent and find another provider because they felt forgotten.

Parent and Caregiver Communication

For pediatric SLP practices, parent communication is essential and time-consuming. AI sends session summaries to parents after each appointment — what was worked on, what to practice at home, progress toward goals — without requiring you to draft individual emails. Parents are better informed. Home practice improves. Outcomes improve. And you are not spending an extra hour on family communication daily.

Progress Report Generation

Quarterly progress reports for school districts, insurance companies, and referring physicians are significant time commitments. AI generates draft progress reports from your session data — goal status, assessment scores, measurable progress metrics, updated treatment recommendations — that you review and finalize. What used to take 45-60 minutes per patient takes 15.

Appointment Reminder and No-Show Reduction

SLP no-shows are expensive — a missed pediatric session cannot always be billed, and the slot is wasted. AI sends reminders 48 hours and 2 hours before every appointment, with rescheduling options built in. No-show rates drop to under 5%. Your schedule stays productive.

Michigan SLP Practice Considerations

School-Based Versus Private Practice

Michigan school-based SLPs face different documentation requirements than private practice — IEP documentation, Medicaid School Aid billing, and IDEA compliance timelines. AI systems can be configured for school-based documentation formats, generating compliant IEP goal progress notes and Medicaid billing documentation aligned with Michigan Medicaid billing requirements.

Telepractice and Hybrid Delivery

Michigan SLP telepractice expanded significantly post-pandemic and has remained a significant delivery modality. AI supports hybrid practices — managing scheduling across in-person and telehealth slots, sending platform links automatically, and maintaining the same documentation quality for remote sessions.

The Private Practice Growth Equation

A Michigan SLP considering whether to expand from solo practice to a group practice faces a documentation burden that scales with every clinician they add. AI creates the infrastructure for sustainable growth — documentation assistance for every clinician, centralized scheduling, and practice management automation — so adding a second or third clinician adds revenue without adding proportional administrative burden. The practices that invest in AI infrastructure now are building businesses that scale cleanly.

HIPAA Compliance by Design

Every AI system we build for Michigan SLP practices operates on HIPAA-compliant infrastructure with Business Associate Agreements, encrypted data handling, access controls, and audit logging. Session data, patient records, and insurance documentation are protected appropriately. Your compliance burden decreases when you replace informal email-based processes with structured AI-driven workflows that maintain proper documentation trails.
