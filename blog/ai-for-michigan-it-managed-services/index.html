Where Michigan MSPs Lose Time and Revenue

Tier-1 Ticket Triage and Resolution

Password resets. Printer connections. VPN issues. Software installation requests. These tickets consume your help desk's time and prevent your technicians from working on complex, billable projects. AI handles tier-1 triage — asking clarifying questions, running automated resolution scripts, escalating only what genuinely requires human attention — while keeping the client informed throughout.

QBR Preparation

A well-run quarterly business review builds client loyalty and surfaces upsell opportunities. But preparing a QBR deck takes 8–12 hours per client — pulling ticket data, uptime metrics, security scan results, and license counts, then formatting them into something presentable. AI generates a draft QBR deck automatically, pulling from your PSA and RMM tools, in under 20 minutes.

Contract and License Renewal Tracking

Every client has Microsoft licenses, antivirus renewals, firewall subscriptions, and backup service contracts — all with different renewal dates. When renewals slip through, you lose margin and credibility. AI tracks every renewal across every client, sends you alerts 90 days out, and drafts the renewal proposal automatically.

New Business Follow-Up

A prospect fills out your website form or meets you at a chamber event. You send an intro email and then get buried in tickets. Three weeks later, they've signed with a competitor. AI runs your new business follow-up sequence — personalized emails at day 1, 3, 7, 14, and 30 — keeping you visible without requiring your attention.

What AI Automates for Michigan MSPs

Intelligent Ticket Triage

Incoming tickets are analyzed, categorized, and routed automatically. Common tier-1 issues trigger automated resolution workflows — password resets via secure link, printer driver installation scripts, VPN configuration checks. The client gets a response in minutes. If automation can't resolve it, a human-ready ticket with full context lands in the right technician's queue.

Automated QBR Reporting

AI pulls data from your PSA (ConnectWise, Autotask, HaloPSA), RMM (N-able, Datto, Ninja), and security tools to generate a complete QBR draft: ticket volume trends, uptime statistics, security posture summary, upcoming renewals, and recommended next steps. Your account manager reviews and adds talking points — they don't build it from scratch.

Renewal Management System

Every license, subscription, and contract end date is tracked in one system. 90 days out: AI flags the renewal and drafts a renewal proposal. 60 days out: renewal email sent to client with current pricing and upgrade options. 30 days out: escalation to account manager if no response. Zero renewals missed. Zero margin left on the table.

Client Health Scoring

AI monitors ticket volume trends, response time satisfaction, payment patterns, and engagement levels to produce a client health score. Declining health scores trigger proactive outreach before the client is at risk. You see churn coming 90 days early instead of finding out when they call to cancel.

Prospect Nurture Sequences

New leads get an automated welcome, a capabilities overview, case studies relevant to their industry, and a soft close — all personalized, all tracked. Hot leads get flagged for direct outreach. Cold leads stay in a long-term nurture sequence. No prospects fall through.
