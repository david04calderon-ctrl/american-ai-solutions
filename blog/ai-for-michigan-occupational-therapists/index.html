Where Michigan OT Practices Lose Time and Revenue

Intake Documentation

New patient intake involves collecting demographics, insurance information, medical history, functional limitations, and physician referral details — before you've even started the evaluation. AI-powered intake forms collect everything digitally before the first appointment, pre-fill your documentation system, and verify insurance eligibility automatically. Your evaluation starts at minute zero — not minute 20.

Prior Authorization Tracking

Michigan's insurance landscape — Blue Cross Blue Shield, Priority Health, Meridian, Molina, United, Aetna — requires prior authorization for many OT services. Tracking open authorizations, renewal dates, and approved visit counts manually is a full-time job in any practice with more than 30 active patients. AI tracks every authorization, sends alerts before visits run out, and triggers renewal requests automatically.

Home Program Delivery and Follow-Up

Home programs are the bridge between your clinic sessions and patient progress. But creating custom home programs, sending them to patients in accessible formats, and following up on adherence takes time your schedule doesn't allow. AI generates structured home programs from your treatment plan inputs, delivers them via email or SMS, and sends automatic check-in messages between sessions.

Scheduling and No-Show Management

OT appointment no-shows are revenue losses that can't be recovered. Without systematic reminders and cancellation management, practices lose 8–15% of billable hours to no-shows. AI sends reminders at 72 hours, 24 hours, and 2 hours before each appointment — with easy confirmation and rescheduling options that reduce no-shows by over 60%.

HIPAA Note: All AI systems built for Michigan OT practices are designed to HIPAA standards — on-premise or compliant cloud architecture, BAA in place, no patient data used for AI training. We build healthcare AI correctly, not quickly.

What AI Automates for Michigan OT Practices

Digital Intake and Insurance Verification

Patients complete a comprehensive intake form before their first visit. AI captures the data, checks insurance eligibility, identifies co-pay and deductible status, and creates a draft patient record — all before they walk in the door. Your evaluation starts with complete information.

Authorization Tracking Dashboard

Every active patient's authorization status is tracked automatically. Visits remaining, expiration date, and payer contact information are visible in one dashboard. When a patient has 3 visits remaining, AI triggers the renewal request. No more discovering at the desk that the authorization expired last week.

Home Program Generator

You input the treatment goals and exercise categories. AI generates a formatted home program with clear instructions, visual cues, frequency guidance, and progression criteria — in plain language the patient and family can follow. Programs are delivered via email or patient portal link, with a read receipt so you know they received it.

Between-Session Check-Ins

Midway between appointments, AI sends a brief check-in message: "How are your home exercises going? Any questions or concerns before Thursday's session?" Patients who are struggling respond early. You arrive at the next session informed — and the patient feels supported between visits, which improves adherence and outcomes.

Appointment Reminders and Rescheduling

Automated reminders at 72 hours, 24 hours, and 2 hours include a one-click confirmation and an easy rescheduling link. Cancellations trigger an automatic offer for the next available slot. Your schedule stays full without your front desk making 20 reminder calls per day.
