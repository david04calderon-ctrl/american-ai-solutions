Michigan Winters Make Windows and Doors a High-Urgency Purchase

Michigan homeowners feel every failed window seal and drafty door frame from October through April. That urgency drives high-intent leads — homeowners who are ready to buy and comparing contractors simultaneously. The window (pun intended) is narrow: most decisions are made within 7–10 days of the first inquiry. AI ensures you're the contractor with the fastest response and the most consistent follow-up.

A Michigan window contractor closing just 2 additional projects per month from improved follow-up adds $201,600 in annual gross revenue at an $8,400 average project value.

Instant Lead Response

Every lead form, website contact, HomeAdvisor inquiry, and Google LSA lead gets an AI response within 60 seconds: "Hi [name] — thanks for reaching out about your windows. We're scheduling free in-home measurements this week. Does [day] or [day] work for you?" Leads that get this response book appointments at 3x the rate of those who wait for a callback.

Quote Follow-Up Sequences

After a quote is delivered, AI runs a 5-touch follow-up sequence over 21 days:

  • Day 1: "Your quote is in your inbox — any questions about the products or installation timeline?"
  • Day 3: Energy savings education — Michigan's heating costs make Energy Star windows a 12–15 year payback with utility rebates
  • Day 7: Financing message — "DTE and Consumers Energy offer rebates for qualifying windows. We also offer 12 months same-as-cash financing."
  • Day 14: "We have an installation slot in [month] — want to lock it in before the season fills up?"
  • Day 21: Final follow-up with a scheduling incentive

Michigan Energy Rebate Campaigns

DTE Energy and Consumers Energy both offer rebates for qualifying window replacements — $1–$4 per square foot for Energy Star-certified products. AI includes rebate information in every quote follow-up and sends a targeted campaign to prospects: "Did you know Michigan homeowners can get $300–$800 back on qualifying window replacements through DTE/Consumers Energy? Your quote qualifies." This converts fence-sitters who were worried about price.

Post-Install Review and Referral Automation

48 hours after installation, AI sends a review request. Two weeks later: a referral ask with a neighbor discount offer. Window replacements are neighborhood purchases — when one home on a street upgrades, neighbors notice. AI turns each completed job into a neighborhood prospecting campaign.

Seasonal Campaign Timing

  • August–September: "Beat the winter — install before October and avoid the holiday rush"
  • February–March: "Spring is the best time to replace — no winter weather delays, summer pricing locks in now"
  • Tax season (Feb–April): "Use your tax refund on windows — 179D deduction may apply for qualifying home improvements"
