Where Michigan Title Companies Lose Time and Money

Manual Order Intake

Every new order requires collecting the same information: parties, property address, purchase price, loan type, lender contact, agent contacts. When intake is manual, data entry errors and missing fields cause downstream delays. AI-powered intake forms capture everything at once — validated, organized, and routed to the right file automatically.

Status Update Phone Tag

Agents and lenders call constantly asking "where are we on this file?" Your processors spend hours answering status calls instead of processing files. AI sends automated status updates at each milestone — title search ordered, commitment issued, curative items listed, clear to close — so your phone stays quiet.

Curative Item Tracking

Open liens, boundary issues, easements, estate matters — curative work is the hardest part of closing management. Items get lost in email. Deadlines slip. Closings get pushed. AI tracks every open item with assigned owners, due dates, and automatic escalation when deadlines approach.

Post-Close Follow-Up and Recording Confirmation

After the table closes, the work isn't done — recording confirmation, final policy delivery, 1099-S filing, and agent relationship maintenance all need attention. AI handles all of this automatically, building the referral relationships that drive repeat business.

Michigan Title Reality: The top referral source for any title company is real estate agents. Agents refer to companies that communicate proactively, close on time, and make their lives easy. AI gives you a systematic communication advantage over every competitor.

What AI Automates for Michigan Title Companies

Intelligent Order Intake

Web-based intake forms collect all required information upfront. AI validates completeness, flags missing fields, and routes the order to the correct processor based on property type, county, and loan type. New orders are in your system with a complete file in minutes — not hours.

Automated Milestone Notifications

At each stage — title search ordered, search received, commitment issued, curative items identified, curative cleared, CD prepared, clear to close, table set, recorded, policies mailed — AI sends automatic updates to all parties via email and SMS. Agents and lenders know exactly where their file stands without calling.

Curative Workflow Management

Every curative item gets assigned to a team member with a deadline and automatic follow-up sequence. If a lien release isn't received by day 5, AI sends a reminder. Day 8, escalation. Day 10, alert to the processor and manager. Nothing gets lost. Closings don't get pushed because someone forgot to follow up.

Agent Relationship Automation

Post-close, AI sends a thank-you message to the agent, requests a Google review, and logs the closing in your CRM. Quarterly, it sends market stats and a check-in. Your best referral sources get consistent attention without your team lifting a finger.

Document Request and Collection

Need a payoff letter, survey, or HOA docs? AI sends the request, tracks the response, and follows up automatically. When the document arrives, it's matched to the correct file and the status is updated. Your processors see a clean checklist — not an inbox full of pending requests.
