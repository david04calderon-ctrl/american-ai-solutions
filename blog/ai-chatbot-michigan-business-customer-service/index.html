The Problem with Generic AI Chatbots

Every website platform now offers "add a chatbot in one click." Wix has one. Squarespace has one. Intercom, Tidio, Drift — all have AI chat features. So why aren't Michigan small businesses seeing results from them?

Because generic chatbots give generic answers.

They're trained on broad internet data, not on your pricing, your service area, your specific job types, your process, or your team's way of talking. Ask one about your emergency service rates and it'll either make something up (hallucination) or say "I don't have that information." Either response destroys trust.

Generic Off-the-Shelf Chatbot

  • Trained on generic data, not your business
  • Hallucinates pricing, policies, and service details
  • Can't book appointments in your actual calendar
  • Frustrates prospects with "I don't know" loops
  • No connection to your CRM or lead pipeline
  • Looks like a bolt-on, not part of your brand

  • Voice and tone matches your brand

That's right in our wheelhouse — we've done a lot of warehouse retrofits in southeast Michigan. For a project that size, our team would start with a no-cost site assessment. I can book that directly for you — what does your schedule look like this week or next?

That entire conversation happened at 11:15 PM. By 7 AM, the owner had a confirmed site assessment on his calendar and the prospect's contact info in HubSpot. No one had to be awake for it.

Healthcare Warning: Off-the-Shelf Chatbots Are a HIPAA Risk

If you're a Michigan medical practice, dental office, or behavioral health provider: standard consumer chatbots (Drift, Tidio, Intercom) do NOT have Business Associate Agreements and are not HIPAA-compliant. Collecting patient information through a non-compliant chatbot is an OCR violation. Every chatbot we build for healthcare clients is architected for HIPAA compliance from day one — including BAA coverage, no PHI in chat logs, and on-premise routing options.

How We Build It: The 4-Step Process

  1. Business knowledge mapping (Week 1)

    We document your services, pricing tiers, service area, FAQs, objections, escalation rules, and the exact voice and tone your brand uses. This becomes the chatbot's knowledge base — it can only answer questions from this data, which eliminates hallucinations.

  2. Integration setup (Week 1–2)

    We connect the chatbot to your calendar (Calendly or direct), your CRM (typically HubSpot), and your notification system. Every captured lead flows into your pipeline automatically with a full conversation transcript.

  3. Test and calibration (Week 2)

    We run hundreds of simulated conversations covering edge cases, out-of-scope questions, and escalation triggers. The chatbot learns when to answer, when to say it doesn't know, and when to route to a human — in that priority order.

  4. Deploy and monitor (Week 3 + ongoing)

    We go live on your website and set up monitoring dashboards so you can see every conversation, every lead captured, and every escalation. Monthly reviews let us update the knowledge base as your business evolves.

Going PRO Training Angle

When we deploy an AI chatbot for your business, we also train your team on how to use the conversation data — reading chat transcripts to understand objections, using captured leads in HubSpot, and updating the knowledge base. The One Thing You Should Not Do

Don't go to Wix, Squarespace, or your website platform's built-in AI chat, turn it on, and assume it's working. Test it yourself. Ask it your three most common customer questions. Ask it your pricing. Ask it something you don't know the answer to.

If it gets any of those wrong, it's damaging your brand every time it talks to a prospect. A bad chatbot is worse than no chatbot — it signals to the customer that you don't care about their experience.

A good chatbot, built on your actual business knowledge, signals the opposite: this company has their act together. That impression matters especially in markets where trust is a deciding factor — and in Michigan construction, healthcare, and automotive supply, trust is everything.
