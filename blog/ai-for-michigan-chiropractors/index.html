What AI Automates for Michigan Chiropractic Clinics

1. Patient Intake and Insurance Verification

New patients complete digital intake forms before their first appointment — chief complaint, pain history, functional limitations, prior treatment, insurance information. AI verifies chiropractic coverage, checks visit limits and copays, and flags Michigan auto no-fault cases for special documentation handling. You walk into the initial exam fully briefed — no front-desk intake bottleneck.

2. SOAP Note and Functional Outcome Documentation

After each patient visit, AI generates a complete SOAP note from structured post-visit input. Michigan auto no-fault cases get functional outcome measure documentation (PROMIS, Neck Disability Index, Oswestry) automatically embedded in the note. Notes are formatted for ChiroTouch, Genesis Chiropractic Software, or Jane App. Review and e-sign in under 5 minutes per patient.

3. Insurance Billing and Claim Submission

AI generates superbills with correct CPT codes (98940, 98941, 98942 based on region count, 97124, 97530), verifies ICD-10 code pairing for clean claim requirements, and submits claims to the appropriate payer. Rejected claims trigger an auto-review flag with rejection reason and correction suggestion. Michigan auto no-fault billing (submitted to the patient's own insurance carrier) gets specific handling built in.

4. Patient Re-Activation Campaigns

Patients who completed a care plan and haven't scheduled in 90 days are your warmest leads. AI sends a re-activation sequence: "We noticed you haven't been in since [date]. How is your [chief complaint] doing?" Patients who respond get scheduling links. Re-activation campaigns convert 15–25% of inactive patients without a single phone call from staff.

5. Care Plan Compliance Reminders

Patients who miss appointments derail care plans and reduce outcomes — and outcomes documentation. AI sends appointment reminders at 48 hours and 4 hours before each visit. When a patient misses, a rescheduling prompt goes out within 2 hours. Compliance improves. Outcomes improve. Documentation of consistent care improves — which is critical for Michigan auto no-fault and workers' comp cases where treatment consistency is scrutinized.

Michigan Auto No-Fault Intelligence: Michigan's post-2020 no-fault reform changed how chiropractic care is covered for auto accident patients. PIP tier selection now determines unlimited vs. capped coverage. AI intake workflows capture PIP tier information, track visit limits by tier, and alert the office when a patient approaches their coverage ceiling — before coverage lapses mid-treatment and leaves the clinic holding uncollectable balances.
