The Three Places Michigan Chiropractic Practices Leak Revenue

1. New Patient Inquiries That Go Cold

Someone visits your website, fills out a contact form, or calls and leaves a voicemail — and doesn't hear back for 24 hours. By then, they've booked with someone else. AI responds within minutes, answers common questions, and gets them on the schedule before they move on.

2. No-Shows and Late Cancellations

No-shows cost Michigan chiropractic practices an average of $80–$180 per slot. AI sends automated reminders via text 48 hours and 24 hours before each appointment — with a one-tap confirm link — cutting no-show rates by 40–60%.

3. Lapsed Patients Who Haven't Returned

Most practices have hundreds of patients who came in for an acute issue, got better, and disappeared. AI identifies patients who haven't booked in 60–90 days and sends a personalized re-engagement sequence — "checking in on your back," not a generic blast. These campaigns convert at 15–25%.

What an AI System for a Michigan Chiropractic Practice Looks Like

We build end-to-end workflows that connect your scheduling system, CRM, and communication channels. Here's what runs automatically:

  • Instant inquiry response: New patient fills out your website form → AI texts and emails them within 2 minutes with a scheduling link
  • Appointment reminders: 48-hour and 24-hour automated texts with confirm/reschedule links
  • Post-visit follow-up: "How are you feeling?" message 3 days after treatment — builds relationship and catches issues before they lead to a lost patient
  • Lapsed patient re-engagement: Identifies no-shows at 60/90/120 days and sends a personalized check-in sequence
  • Review requests: After the 3rd visit, automatically asks satisfied patients to leave a Google review
  • Care plan reminders: Patients on multi-visit plans get automated reminders tied to their care schedule
