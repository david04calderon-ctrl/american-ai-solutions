Six AI Automations Built for Michigan Chiropractic Practices

Patient Reactivation Sequences

AI identifies patients who haven't visited in 60, 90, or 120 days and launches personalized reactivation outreach — referencing their specific condition, last visit findings, and how maintenance care typically helps long-term. Recovers 25–40% of lapsed patients within 45 days.

SOAP Note Assistance

AI generates SOAP note drafts from voice dictation or structured prompts after each adjustment — capturing subjective findings, objective measurements, assessment, and plan in the format required by your EHR and insurance carriers. DC reviews, edits, and signs. Reduces documentation time by 50–65%.

Auto No-Fault Billing Support

Michigan's PIP system requires specific documentation timelines and billing codes. AI tracks each auto injury case's documentation requirements, flags cases approaching filing deadlines, and ensures all required narrative reports and functional outcome measures are completed on schedule. Reduces PIP claim denials by 60–80%.

Care Plan Compliance Communication

When a patient is prescribed a 12-visit care plan, AI sends milestone communications — motivational messages after visits 3, 6, and 9 that acknowledge progress and reinforce the clinical rationale for completing the plan. Care plan completion rates improve 20–35% with consistent communication.

Insurance Verification & Authorization

AI verifies insurance eligibility and benefits 48 hours before each appointment, checks for prior authorization requirements (common with Blue Cross, Priority Health, and Medicaid), and flags coverage issues before the patient arrives. Eliminates day-of billing surprises and reduces AR days.

Referral Thank-You & Relationship Automation

When a patient refers a family member or friend, AI sends a personalized thank-you to the referring patient within 24 hours. Referring MD and specialist relationships tracked and acknowledged monthly with practice update communications. Systematizes the referral relationships that drive chiropractic growth.

Michigan Auto No-Fault: The Unique Billing Opportunity

Michigan's auto no-fault system deserves special attention. Michigan Public Act 21 of 2019 restructured the system with tiered benefit options, but for accidents before July 2020 and for patients with unlimited PIP coverage, chiropractic care is among the most generously covered services in any state.

Michigan Auto No-Fault Chiropractic Billing Requirements

PIP Documentation Standards: Michigan auto insurers require contemporaneous documentation — SOAP notes must be completed within 24–48 hours of each visit for PIP billing. AI SOAP documentation systems help ensure every visit is documented on time, reducing the most common PIP denial reason.

Functional Outcome Measures: Many Michigan auto insurers now require standardized functional outcome measures (Oswestry, Neck Disability Index, DASH) at regular intervals. AI can schedule and track these measurements automatically and flag when reassessment is due.

IME Preparation: Independent Medical Examinations are common in Michigan PIP cases. AI can compile a complete case summary from all SOAP notes, treatment records, and outcome measures — formatted for IME review — in minutes rather than hours.

HIPAA Compliance: All AI systems handling patient records are business associates under HIPAA. Signed BAAs required. No patient data to consumer AI platforms.

Michigan Board of Chiropractic (LARA): Michigan chiropractors are licensed under MCL 333.16401 et seq. AI cannot provide chiropractic recommendations — all clinical decisions require licensed DC judgment.
