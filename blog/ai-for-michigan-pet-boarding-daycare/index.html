Michigan Pet Families Board Multiple Times Per Year — The Question Is Whether They Come Back to You

Michigan's vacation patterns — UP trips in summer, holiday travel in November and December, spring break, and long weekends — create 3–5 boarding occasions per pet family per year. A facility with 40 kennels fully booked at peak periods can generate $400,000+ annually. But the difference between 60% and 90% occupancy is almost entirely about retention — and retention is almost entirely about follow-up.

Moving from 60% to 80% kennel occupancy on a 40-kennel facility adds $150,000+ in annual gross revenue. AI-driven rebooking, holiday outreach, and referral campaigns are the fastest path to that occupancy improvement.

Post-Stay Rebooking Sequences

Two days after pickup, AI sends: "How is [pet's name] settling back in at home? We loved having them! Michigan summer is filling up fast — want to pre-book your next trip now to lock in your spot?" Pet families who pre-book after a stay return at 4x the rate of those who don't. The emotional window right after pickup — when they're happy with the care — is the optimal conversion moment.

Holiday Capacity Campaigns

Michigan boarding peaks at Thanksgiving, Christmas, Memorial Day, Fourth of July, and Labor Day. AI sends targeted campaigns 6–8 weeks before each holiday:

  • October (pre-Thanksgiving): "Thanksgiving boarding fills up by early November — reserve [pet's name]'s spot now"
  • November (pre-Christmas): "The holidays are our busiest time — December is almost full. Want to lock in your dates?"
  • April (pre-summer): "Summer travel season is coming — Memorial Day and July 4th book weeks in advance"

Facilities using holiday advance campaigns fill 85%+ of holiday capacity weeks earlier — and at full rate, not discounted last-minute.

Daycare Membership Conversion

Pet families who board are natural daycare candidates — their dog is already comfortable in your facility. After a second boarding stay, AI sends: "Since [pet's name] loves being here, have you thought about daycare? Our monthly daycare membership is $[X] and includes [days/week] — most members find their dog is calmer, better socialized, and easier to leave on travel days." Daycare membership creates weekly recurring revenue from your highest-trust customers.

New Pet Family Welcome Sequences

For first-time boarders, AI sends a pre-stay welcome sequence: vaccination reminder checklist, drop-off instructions, and a "what to expect" guide that reduces anxiety and no-shows. After the stay, the post-care sequence captures feedback and pre-books the next visit before the pet family has a chance to look elsewhere.

Referral Campaigns

Michigan pet owners are deeply connected in neighborhood Facebook groups, breed communities, and dog parks. AI sends a referral message two weeks after a completed stay: "If any of your pet-owner friends are looking for boarding, send them our way — their first night is complimentary, and you get a free night on your next stay." Boarding referrals convert at 55%+ because the trust transfer is high — leaving your pet with someone is the highest-stakes recommendation a pet owner can make.
