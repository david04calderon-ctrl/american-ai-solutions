The Revenue Michigan Dental Practices Leave on the Table

1. Patients Overdue for Recall

Most dental practices have hundreds of patients who are 6, 12, or 18+ months overdue for a cleaning. AI identifies each one, sends a personalized recall message at the right time — "It's been 8 months since your last visit — your dental benefits may be about to reset" — and follows up at 2 and 4 weeks if there's no response. Recall compliance improves 30–45%.

2. Appointment No-Shows and Last-Minute Cancellations

A dental chair sitting empty is $200–$400 per hour in lost revenue. AI sends appointment reminders 48 hours and 24 hours before every appointment with a confirm link. No-show rates drop 40–55%. When someone cancels, AI immediately offers the slot to the next patient on the short-call list.

3. Treatment Plan Follow-Up

A patient is diagnosed with a crown or a deep cleaning. They leave without scheduling the follow-up appointment. AI sends a treatment plan reminder 1 week and 3 weeks after the diagnosis — "We wanted to check in on the treatment plan we discussed. Do you have any questions?" Treatment acceptance rates improve 20–30%.

What the AI System Handles for Michigan Dental Practices

  • Appointment reminders: 48-hour and 24-hour text reminders with confirm/reschedule link
  • Recall campaigns: Automated sequences for patients overdue at 6, 9, 12, and 18 months
  • Treatment plan follow-up: 1-week and 3-week reminders for unscheduled treatment
  • Insurance benefit reminders: October/November campaign — "use your dental benefits before they reset"
  • New patient welcome sequence: Post-first-visit follow-up and 6-month recall pre-booking
  • Review requests: Sent after positive appointment experiences
  • Referral asks: Annual "do you know someone looking for a dentist?" to loyal patients
Michigan Dental Insurance Note: Michigan has very high dental benefit utilization rates — BCBSM, Delta Dental, MetLife, and Guardian are dominant. Most Michigan families have benefits that reset January 1. A December "use it before you lose it" campaign to patients with unused benefits is one of the highest-ROI campaigns in dental AI. We build and run it automatically every year.
