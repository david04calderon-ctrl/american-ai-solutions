The AI System Built for Garage Door Companies

1. 24/7 Emergency Call Capture

AI answers every call — day, night, weekend, holiday. It captures the problem, the address, the urgency level, and the customer's phone number. For genuine emergencies (broken spring, door won't open or close, vehicle trapped), it immediately texts your on-call tech with the job details. For non-emergencies, it schedules a callback or sends a quote request form. You never miss a job again.

2. Instant Quote Delivery

AI sends a quote within 60 seconds of a service request — broken spring replacement, opener installation, tune-up, new door estimate. The quote includes your pricing, expected arrival window, and a "Book Now" link. Customers who receive fast quotes convert at 3–4× the rate of those waiting 24+ hours for a callback.

3. Seasonal Maintenance Campaigns

AI identifies every customer who hasn't had a tune-up in 12 months and automatically sends a spring campaign in March and a pre-winter campaign in September. These campaigns consistently generate $8,000–$15,000 in booked tune-ups and lubricant treatments per campaign — from customers you already own.

4. Review Request Automation

48 hours after job completion, AI sends a personalized review request to every customer. Michigan garage door companies with 50+ Google reviews win significantly more calls from Google Maps searches. AI builds that review base on autopilot.

5. Replacement Door Follow-Up

Every customer who called for a repair gets a 90-day follow-up about door replacement options — new insulated steel doors, wood composite, modern glass designs. These upsell conversations convert at 12–18% when timed right and personalized to their service history.
