Michigan's weather makes car washes one of the most reliable service businesses in the state. Salt season alone drives massive demand from November through March. The challenge for Michigan car wash owners isn't getting customers — it's converting them into members, keeping those members, and filling slow periods between weather events.

AI automates all three.

The Membership Model Is Where the Real Money Is

A single-visit customer pays $12–$20 per wash and comes in 15–20 times per year — roughly $250 annually. An unlimited monthly member pays $25–$45/month and generates $300–$540 per year with significantly more visits — and predictable recurring revenue.

The problem: most car washes don't have a systematic way to convert single-visit customers into members. They put up a sign at the POS and hope. AI builds a conversion sequence that follows up after the first visit and makes the membership pitch with a compelling offer.

Member Conversion Sequence

Customer uses the car wash once. AI sends a follow-up text within 24 hours: "Thanks for visiting! Michigan winters are brutal — our unlimited members save an average of $180/year. Here's a first-month discount to get started." Conversion rate from this single sequence: 8–15% of single-visit customers.

Churn Reduction

Members cancel for two reasons: they moved, or they forgot they had it. AI identifies members who haven't scanned in for 30+ days and sends a re-engagement message before they cancel. A significant portion were meaning to come but hadn't gotten around to it.

Seasonal Campaigns

Michigan has predictable demand spikes — first warm days in March/April, back-to-school August, pre-winter October. AI runs targeted campaigns to non-members in your database during these windows with time-limited offers. These convert 10–18% when timed right.

What the AI System Manages

  • Post-visit membership pitch: Triggered after first wash — personalized offer, easy sign-up link
  • Inactivity detection: Members not scanning in for 30+ days get a check-in message
  • Cancellation save sequence: When a member initiates cancel, AI sends a pause-not-cancel offer
  • Seasonal campaigns: Pre-built for Michigan weather cycles — spring, summer, pre-winter
  • Review requests: Sent to members after their 5th visit to drive Google visibility
  • Referral program: Automated "give a friend a free wash" campaign to grow your member base
Michigan-Specific Note: Michigan roads use more road salt than almost any other state. The 6-month salt season (October–April) creates a built-in urgency that makes membership pitches especially compelling — "protect your car's paint and undercarriage all winter for $29/month" is a message that converts well in Michigan specifically.
