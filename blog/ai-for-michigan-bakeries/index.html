Michigan Bakery Revenue Has Three Layers — Most Shops Only Work One

Walk-in retail is the floor. Catering and special orders are the ceiling. Wholesale and subscription accounts are the foundation. Most Michigan bakeries focus almost entirely on walk-in retail and leave $30,000 to $80,000 per year in catering, wholesale, and subscription revenue on the table because they have no system for developing and retaining those accounts. AI builds all three layers simultaneously.

A Michigan bakery that adds 10 weekly subscription customers at $80 per week adds $41,600 in guaranteed annual revenue before a single walk-in customer, holiday order, or catering contract is included.

Holiday Pre-Order Campaigns

Michigan bakeries that pre-sell holidays in advance have lower waste, better cash flow, and higher revenue per holiday. AI sends pre-order campaigns 3 weeks before each major holiday: "Thanksgiving pies are available for pre-order now. We sell out every year. Order by November 15 to guarantee yours." Pre-order campaigns capture 35 to 50% of holiday revenue before the holiday arrives, eliminating the rush-day scramble.

Catering Inquiry Follow-Up

When a customer inquires about a wedding cake, corporate catering, or event order, AI responds within 60 seconds and sends a follow-up sequence if they do not book: "We got your catering inquiry for [event/date]. Our catering calendar is filling up for that month. Want to schedule a quick tasting call this week?" Catering follow-up sequences recover 30 to 45% of stalled inquiries that would otherwise go to a competitor.

Subscription and Weekly Order Programs

After a customer's third purchase, AI sends a subscription offer: "You have ordered from us 3 times now. Want to set up a weekly order? We can have your [favorites] ready for pickup every [day] at a 10% discount. Most of our regulars say it is one less thing to think about." Subscription conversion after 3 purchases runs 25 to 35%.

Corporate Account Development

Michigan offices, law firms, hospitals, and event venues order catering regularly. AI sends targeted commercial outreach: "We provide corporate breakfast catering for Michigan businesses — fresh baked goods delivered weekly for team meetings, client events, and office standups. Free sample delivery for new accounts. Want to see our corporate menu?" Corporate catering accounts average $600 to $2,400 per month and renew automatically.

Past Customer Reactivation

Customers who bought once and have not returned within 60 days are high-risk churn. AI sends a reactivation campaign: "We miss you. Here is what is new in the bakery this week — plus a 15% off code for your next order. Come see us." Reactivation campaigns bring back 20 to 35% of lapsed customers.

Wedding and Event Pipeline

Michigan wedding season drives significant custom cake and dessert revenue. AI manages the wedding inquiry pipeline: instant response to cake consultation requests, gallery-sharing sequences, and follow-up after tastings. Wedding cake orders average $800 to $2,500 and drive ancillary dessert table and favor orders that add $400 to $900 per event.
