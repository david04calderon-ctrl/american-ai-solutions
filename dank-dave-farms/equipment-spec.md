## Lighting — HLG (Horticulture Lighting Group)

HLG is the gold standard for LED horticulture lighting. Samsung LM301H/LM301B diodes, proven by commercial and craft grows worldwide.

| 2× | **HLG Diablo 650R** | 650W ea | 4×4–5×4 ea | ~$899 ea |
| — | Total draw | 1,300W | 10×8 coverage | $1,798 |

**Why Diablo 650R:**
- Top-bin Samsung LM301H EVO diodes
- Patented Refraction Lens Technology (RLT) for deep canopy penetration

- 5-year warranty, runs cool enough for basement environment
- Commercial growers stack these in 4×8 trays at 1,300W total — this is the setup

**Alternative (Budget):** Spider Farmer SE7000 (2× at ~$599 ea) — solid performer, slightly below HLG in photon efficiency

## Climate Control

### Primary AC — Mini-Split (Strongly Recommended for 10×10+ Flower)
| Model | BTU | Coverage | Price |
|-------|-----|----------|-------|
| **Mitsubishi MSZ-GL12NA** | 12,000 BTU | Up to 550 sq ft | ~$1,200–1,500 installed |
| Or **Pioneer Diamante 12K** | 12,000 BTU | DIY install option | ~$799 + install |

Mini-split is non-negotiable at 1,300W of LEDs in a 10×10 room. Generates 4,400+ BTU of heat that must be removed.

### Dehumidification
| Model | Pints/Day | Coverage | Price |
|-------|-----------|----------|-------|
| **Quest 335** | 335 pints | Up to 3,000 sq ft | ~$2,299 |
| **Quest 225** | 225 pints | Up to 2,500 sq ft | ~$1,699 |

**Why Quest:** Commercial-grade compressor, designed for grow environments, runs quietly, handles extreme loads in late flower when plants transpire heavily. This is what every serious cultivator uses. Don't cheap out on dehumidification — bud rot kills harvests.

**Alternative (personal grow budget):** AC Infinity AIRTAP T4 Dehumidifier or Inkbird IHC-200-paired unit for smaller basement rooms

### Oscillating Circulation Fans
| Model | Size | Qty | Price |

## Water & Irrigation

### Water Source
- **RO Filter (Reverse Osmosis):** NU Aqua Platinum Series 100 GPD — ~$89
  - Removes chloramine, chlorine, heavy metals, and mineral buildup
  - Bring water to near 0 PPM, then build back up with minerals if needed
  - Living soil does not want chlorine killing the microbiome

### pH & PPM Metering
| Tool | Model | Price |
|------|-------|-------|
| pH meter | **Apera Instruments PC60** (combo meter) | ~$149 |
| TDS/PPM | Built into Apera PC60 | — |
| pH Up/Down | General Hydroponics Up/Down | ~$25 |
| Calibration solution | 4.0, 7.0, 10.0 pH buffer | ~$20 |

pH is calibrated before every watering. Living soil buffer is 6.5–6.8.

### Automated Irrigation (Optional Phase 2)
- **Bluelab Dosetronic** or **Dosatron D14MZ2** — automated nutrient/pH dosing
- **Digital Aquatics Growroom Controller** — full environmental + irrigation control
- For Phase 1, hand watering is preferred (you read the plants)
