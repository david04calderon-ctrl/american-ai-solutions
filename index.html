Common Questions

Everything You Need to Know Before You Call.

How is this different from just giving my team ChatGPT?

ChatGPT is a tool. We build systems. The difference is a workflow that's integrated into your CRM, your email, your ops platform — and runs automatically, without anyone prompting it. We also fine-tune models on your specific data so results are accurate to your business, not generic.

Do we need technical staff to maintain it after you leave?

No. We build for your team's actual capability. Every engagement includes documented SOPs, a team training session, and a 30-day support window. If something breaks or needs updating, we're one email away. We design for operational independence, not dependency on us.

Can we really get grant funding to cover the cost?

Yes — for qualifying Michigan businesses. The Going PRO Talent Fund (Michigan LEO, $6M pool) covers AI training costs. The Industry 4.0 Tech Grant reimburses up to 50% of technology implementation costs for small manufacturers. We help you structure the engagement to qualify and handle the documentation.

What if AI isn't actually the right solution for our business?

We'll tell you. Our audit is honest — if the highest-ROI move is fixing a process before automating it, that's what we recommend. We've turned down engagements where the problem wasn't an AI problem. That honesty is why our clients refer us.

How quickly will we see real results?

First automation live in 3–4 weeks on a Quick Deploy engagement. We schedule an explicit ROI checkpoint at 30 days post-launch — we measure the output against your baseline and put the numbers in writing. You'll know if it's working, and so will we.

Do you only work with Michigan businesses?

Michigan is our priority — we know the market, the OEM supply chain, the grant landscape, and the local business community. We do take select clients outside Michigan when the engagement is a strong fit. Reach out and we'll let you know quickly if it makes sense.
