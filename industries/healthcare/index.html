⚠ Not HIPAA-Compliant

Why ChatGPT puts your practice at risk

OpenAI's standard ChatGPT and most AI tools do not offer a Business Associate Agreement. Using them to process patient data — even to draft a note or look up a code — is a HIPAA violation. OCR enforcement is active and fines start at $100 per violation with no ceiling on willful neglect. We've seen practices try to save money on the tool. We've also seen the audit that followed.
