# Podcast & Speaking Strategy — American AI Solutions LLC
**Goal:** Get on 3–5 Michigan business podcasts and 2–3 speaking events in the first 90 days
**Why:** One 30-minute podcast episode generates more qualified inbound than a month of cold email. The audience is pre-qualified (Michigan business owners) and the trust level is high.

## TALK FRAMEWORKS (HAVE THESE READY)

### Talk 1: "AI for Michigan Business Owners — No Hype, Just Results" (25 min)

**Audience:** General Michigan business community (TechTown, Chamber)
**Structure:**
- **Hook (2 min):** The difference between AI hype and AI that moves the P&L
- **The Michigan opportunity (5 min):** Why now? OEM mandates, grant funding, labor costs
- **The 3 automations that actually work (10 min):** Quality inspection, workflow automation, communication AI — one Michigan example per category, real numbers
- **The grant nobody's using (3 min):** Going PRO + Industry 4.0 — concrete eligibility criteria
- **How to evaluate if AI is right for you (3 min):** 5-question self-assessment
- **Q&A (5 min)**

### Talk 2: "OEM AI Mandates: What Michigan Tier 2/3 Suppliers Need to Know" (25 min)

**Audience:** Manufacturing associations, MMA events, automotive-focused venues
**Structure:**
- What Ford, GM, and Stellantis are actually requiring (with citations)
- The 5-level Industry 4.0 maturity model — where most Michigan suppliers are vs. where OEMs want them
- The fastest path from Level 1 to Level 3 AI readiness
- What it costs and how to fund it (grants + ROI timeline)
- Q&A focused on their specific situation

### Talk 3: "HIPAA and AI: What Michigan Healthcare Practices Are Getting Wrong" (20 min)

**Audience:** Healthcare association events, medical director forums
**Structure:**
- The HIPAA myth that's costing Michigan practices 20 hours/week
- What Anthropic, Microsoft, and Google actually offer (BAA details)
- The 3 workflows where AI ROI is fastest in a healthcare practice
- How to start: a 2-week pilot structure that proves value before commitment
- Q&A

## AFTER EVERY APPEARANCE: THE REPURPOSING STACK

One podcast episode or speaking event = many pieces of content:

1. **LinkedIn post:** Key insight from the talk/episode, link to listen (day of release)
2. **LinkedIn carousel:** Turn the 3 main points into a carousel PDF
3. **Email to your list:** "I was on [Podcast] this week — here's the key thing I shared"
4. **Quote card:** One-liner pulled from the episode → branded graphic → LinkedIn
5. **Blog post:** Turn the talk transcript into a blog article (SEO value)
6. **Clips:** If video, request clips from the host — share on LinkedIn and Instagram

A 30-minute podcast appearance can produce 5–7 pieces of content that extend its reach over 2–4 weeks.
