## HOW TO USE LOOM

1. Go to **loom.com** → Sign up free → Install the browser extension (Chrome) or desktop app
2. Click the Loom icon → Select "Screen + Camera"
3. Make sure your camera shows in the corner (bottom left by default)
4. Open your browser to americanaisolutionsllc.com — have it showing, you don't need to talk about it, just let it be visible
5. Click **Start Recording** → read the script naturally (it's okay to glance at notes off-screen — looks authentic)
6. Stop recording → Loom auto-saves and gives you a shareable link immediately
7. Copy the link → paste it into your outreach emails

**Loom settings to enable:**
- Call to action button → "Book a Strategy Call" → link to Calendly
- Password protection: OFF (you want them to watch without friction)
- Auto-generated transcript: ON (Loom adds captions for accessibility)
- Custom thumbnail: click the thumbnail → pick the best frame

## RECORDING TIPS

**Before you record:**
- Close Slack, email notifications, anything that might pop up
- Charge your laptop or plug in (don't let the battery warning appear)
- Test your audio — use headphones with a mic if possible (airpods, jabra, anything beats the built-in mic)
- Drink water right before — a dry mouth is audible

**During the record:**
- Speak slightly slower than feels natural — you sound faster on camera than in person
- Look at the camera lens, not your own face in the preview
- It's fine to restart. If you stumble badly, stop, click "Discard and re-record"
- One re-take is usually enough — don't over-polish it. Authentic > perfect.

**The 8-second rule:** The first 8 seconds must contain a name + what you do + why they should care. The script above is built around this. Don't start with "Hey, um, so I just wanted to introduce myself..."
