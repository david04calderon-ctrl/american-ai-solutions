# Client Master Services Agreement (MSA) Template
# American AI Solutions LLC

**Purpose:** This is the legal agreement every client signs before project work begins. The MSA governs the entire relationship — IP ownership, payment, confidentiality, warranties, liability limits, and dispute resolution. The SOW (Statement of Work) is an exhibit to this agreement that defines the specific project scope.
**How to use:** Send this via Pandadoc after the proposal is accepted. Client signs the MSA + the project-specific SOW as Exhibit A. Collect 50% deposit on signature.
**Legal note:** This template was drafted for general consulting engagements in Michigan. Have a Michigan business attorney review it before first use. Cost: $300–$600 for a one-time review. Worth it.

---

# MASTER SERVICES AGREEMENT

This Master Services Agreement ("Agreement") is entered into as of [Date] ("Effective Date") between:

**American AI Solutions LLC**, a Michigan limited liability company ("AAIS"), and

**[Client Company Name]**, a [state] [entity type] ("Client").

Together referred to as the "Parties."

---

## 1. SERVICES

### 1.1 Statements of Work
AAIS will provide the services described in one or more Statements of Work ("SOW") executed by both Parties and attached as exhibits to this Agreement. Each SOW is incorporated into and governed by this Agreement.

### 1.2 Changes to Scope
Any changes to the scope of services defined in an SOW must be agreed to in writing by both Parties via a Change Order signed by authorized representatives of both Parties before additional work commences. AAIS is not obligated to perform work outside the scope of an SOW unless a Change Order is executed.

### 1.3 Client Cooperation
Client agrees to provide timely access to systems, data, personnel, and information reasonably necessary for AAIS to perform the services. Delays caused by Client's failure to provide required access may extend project timelines and, at AAIS's discretion, affect project fees.

---

## 2. FEES AND PAYMENT

### 2.1 Fees
Client agrees to pay AAIS the fees specified in each SOW.

### 2.2 Payment Schedule
Unless otherwise specified in the SOW:
- 50% of the total project fee is due upon execution of the applicable SOW ("Deposit")
- 50% of the total project fee is due upon delivery and acceptance of the final deliverables ("Final Payment")

### 2.3 Invoices
AAIS will issue invoices for amounts due. Invoices are payable within [15] days of receipt.

### 2.4 Late Payments
Amounts not paid within 15 days of the invoice due date accrue interest at 1.5% per month (18% per year) or the maximum rate permitted by Michigan law, whichever is less. AAIS reserves the right to suspend services for accounts more than 30 days past due.

### 2.5 Expenses
Unless specified in the SOW, all reasonable out-of-pocket expenses (travel, software licenses, cloud hosting costs) incurred by AAIS in performing services are billable to Client at cost, with prior written approval for expenses exceeding $500.

---

## 3. INTELLECTUAL PROPERTY

### 3.1 Client-Owned Deliverables
Upon receipt of full payment, AAIS assigns to Client all right, title, and interest in the custom deliverables developed specifically for Client under each SOW ("Deliverables"), including all associated intellectual property rights. For clarity, "Deliverables" means the custom code, workflows, documentation, and system configurations created specifically for Client's project.

### 3.2 AAIS Background IP
AAIS retains all right, title, and interest in its pre-existing tools, frameworks, methodologies, templates, and general-purpose code ("Background IP"), even if incorporated into Deliverables. AAIS grants Client a perpetual, non-exclusive, royalty-free license to use Background IP solely as incorporated in the Deliverables for Client's internal business purposes.

### 3.3 Third-Party Components
Certain deliverables may incorporate third-party software, APIs, or open-source components. Such components are subject to their respective licenses. AAIS will disclose material third-party dependencies in the applicable SOW.

### 3.4 Portfolio Rights
AAIS may reference Client's name and describe the general nature of the engagement in its marketing materials, case studies, and portfolio, subject to Client's prior written approval of any such reference. AAIS will not disclose Client's confidential information without approval.

---

## 4. CONFIDENTIALITY

### 4.1 Definition
"Confidential Information" means any non-public information disclosed by one Party to the other that is designated as confidential or that reasonably should be understood to be confidential given the nature of the information and circumstances of disclosure.

### 4.2 Obligations
Each Party agrees to: (a) hold the other Party's Confidential Information in strict confidence; (b) not disclose such information to any third party without prior written consent; and (c) use such information only for purposes of performing this Agreement.

### 4.3 Exceptions
Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that: (a) is or becomes publicly available through no fault of the receiving Party; (b) was rightfully known to the receiving Party prior to disclosure; (c) is independently developed by the receiving Party without use of the Confidential Information; or (d) is required to be disclosed by law or court order, provided the receiving Party gives prompt written notice to the disclosing Party.

### 4.4 Duration
Confidentiality obligations survive termination of this Agreement for a period of three (3) years.

---

## 5. DATA PROTECTION AND COMPLIANCE

### 5.1 Client Data
Client retains all ownership of its data provided to AAIS. AAIS will use Client data solely to perform services under this Agreement and will not sell, transfer, or otherwise use Client data for any other purpose.

### 5.2 Healthcare Clients — HIPAA
For engagements involving Protected Health Information (PHI) as defined by HIPAA, the Parties will execute a Business Associate Agreement ("BAA") as a separate exhibit to this Agreement prior to any PHI processing. AAIS will implement technical, administrative, and physical safeguards as required by the BAA.

### 5.3 Security
AAIS will implement commercially reasonable security measures to protect Client data. AAIS will notify Client promptly (and in no event later than 72 hours) of any confirmed data breach affecting Client data.

---

## 6. WARRANTIES AND REPRESENTATIONS

### 6.1 AAIS Warranties
AAIS represents and warrants that: (a) it has the right to enter into this Agreement; (b) the services will be performed in a professional and workmanlike manner; (c) the Deliverables will substantially conform to the specifications in the applicable SOW; and (d) to AAIS's knowledge, the Deliverables do not infringe the intellectual property rights of any third party.

### 6.2 Client Warranties
Client represents and warrants that: (a) it has the right to enter into this Agreement; (b) it has all necessary rights and permissions to provide data and systems access to AAIS; and (c) Client's use of the Deliverables will comply with applicable laws.

### 6.3 Disclaimer
EXCEPT AS EXPRESSLY SET FORTH IN THIS AGREEMENT, AAIS PROVIDES SERVICES AND DELIVERABLES "AS IS" AND DISCLAIMS ALL IMPLIED WARRANTIES, INCLUDING WARRANTIES OF MERCHANTABILITY AND FITNESS FOR A PARTICULAR PURPOSE.

---

## 7. LIMITATION OF LIABILITY

### 7.1 Exclusion of Consequential Damages
IN NO EVENT WILL EITHER PARTY BE LIABLE FOR INDIRECT, INCIDENTAL, SPECIAL, CONSEQUENTIAL, OR PUNITIVE DAMAGES, INCLUDING LOST PROFITS OR LOST DATA, ARISING OUT OF OR RELATED TO THIS AGREEMENT, EVEN IF ADVISED OF THE POSSIBILITY OF SUCH DAMAGES.

### 7.2 Aggregate Liability Cap
AAIS's aggregate liability for all claims arising out of or related to this Agreement will not exceed the total fees paid by Client to AAIS in the three (3) months preceding the claim.

### 7.3 Essential Basis
The Parties acknowledge that the limitations in this Section reflect a reasonable allocation of risk and are an essential basis of the bargain between the Parties.

---

## 8. TERM AND TERMINATION

### 8.1 Term
This Agreement commences on the Effective Date and continues until terminated as provided herein.

### 8.2 Termination for Convenience
Either Party may terminate this Agreement with 30 days' written notice. Upon termination for convenience: (a) Client will pay for all services performed through the termination date; (b) AAIS will deliver all completed work product; and (c) the Deposit is non-refundable.

### 8.3 Termination for Cause
Either Party may terminate this Agreement immediately upon written notice if the other Party materially breaches this Agreement and fails to cure such breach within 10 business days after receiving written notice of the breach.

### 8.4 Effect of Termination
Sections 3 (IP), 4 (Confidentiality), 5 (Data), 7 (Liability), and 9 (Dispute Resolution) survive termination.

---

## 9. DISPUTE RESOLUTION

### 9.1 Informal Resolution
The Parties will attempt to resolve any dispute through good-faith negotiation before pursuing formal dispute resolution. Either Party may initiate this process by written notice to the other.

### 9.2 Mediation
If informal resolution fails within 30 days, the Parties will submit to non-binding mediation in Wayne County, Michigan, before a mutually agreed mediator.

### 9.3 Governing Law
This Agreement is governed by the laws of the State of Michigan, without regard to conflict of law principles.

### 9.4 Venue
Any legal action arising out of this Agreement will be brought exclusively in the state or federal courts located in Wayne County, Michigan.

### 9.5 Attorney's Fees
In any action to enforce this Agreement, the prevailing Party is entitled to recover reasonable attorney's fees and costs.

---

## 10. GENERAL PROVISIONS

### 10.1 Independent Contractors
The Parties are independent contractors. Nothing in this Agreement creates an employment, partnership, agency, or joint venture relationship.

### 10.2 Non-Solicitation
During the term of this Agreement and for 12 months thereafter, Client agrees not to solicit for employment or independently engage any AAIS employee or subcontractor who performed services under this Agreement, without AAIS's prior written consent.

### 10.3 Force Majeure
Neither Party is liable for delays or failures caused by events beyond its reasonable control, including natural disasters, government actions, or widespread internet outages, provided the affected Party gives prompt notice.

### 10.4 Entire Agreement
This Agreement, together with all SOWs and exhibits, constitutes the entire agreement between the Parties and supersedes all prior agreements and understandings relating to its subject matter.

### 10.5 Amendment
This Agreement may only be amended by a written document signed by authorized representatives of both Parties.

### 10.6 Waiver
Failure to enforce any provision of this Agreement does not waive the right to enforce it in the future.

### 10.7 Severability
If any provision is found unenforceable, the remaining provisions remain in full effect.

### 10.8 Notices
Notices under this Agreement must be in writing and delivered by email with read receipt to the addresses specified in the applicable SOW, or by certified mail to the Parties' principal business addresses.

### 10.9 Counterparts
This Agreement may be executed in counterparts, including electronic signatures, each of which is an original.

## SETUP IN PANDADOC

1. Create a new template in Pandadoc: "AAIS Master Services Agreement"
2. Add the full MSA text above as the document body
3. Create merge fields for: [Date], [Client Company Name], [Client entity type], [Client state]
4. Add two signature blocks: AAIS (David Calderon, pre-filled) and Client (name, title, date)
5. Attach SOW as a second document in the same Pandadoc envelope
6. Set payment collection in Pandadoc: collect 50% deposit via credit card or ACH upon signature

When sending: "Here's the MSA and SOW for our engagement — everything is pre-filled based on what we discussed. The 50% deposit processes automatically when you sign. Questions before you sign, just reply here."

Keep signing fast. Speed in closing is a competitive advantage.
