# AAIS Faceless Content Scripts
## Cross-Platform: TikTok · Instagram Reels · YouTube Shorts · LinkedIn Video

**Format:** Text-on-screen + voiceover. No face required.
**Tools to produce:** CapCut (free), InVideo AI, Canva Video, Descript
**Target length:** 15–45 seconds per reel
**Posting cadence:** 1/day minimum across all platforms

---

## HOW TO USE THESE SCRIPTS

Each script has:
- **HOOK** — first 1–3 seconds (make or break)
- **BODY** — the proof or story
- **CTA** — what they do next
- **VISUALS** — what to show on screen (B-roll, screen recordings, text overlays)
- **CAPTION** — paste directly into the post
- **HASHTAGS** — platform-specific

For faceless production: Record your screen showing the AI system running, or use stock B-roll of Michigan manufacturing/healthcare/trucks. Drop text overlays. Add voiceover (your own, or AI voice via ElevenLabs/Descript).
