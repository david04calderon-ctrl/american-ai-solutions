# Weekly Execution Dashboard
# American AI Solutions LLC

**Purpose:** This is the single document David opens every Monday morning. It translates every strategy, every playbook, and every system into a concrete weekly action list. The system is only as strong as the weekly execution. Most businesses fail not because of bad strategy — they fail because they stop executing consistently.
**Rule:** Every Monday, spend 15 minutes completing this dashboard. Every Friday, score the week. 12 consistent weeks beats 1 perfect quarter.

---

## THE MONDAY MORNING RITUAL (15 minutes)

Every Monday, go through this checklist in order:

### 1. Pipeline Review (5 min)
Open HubSpot. Check every deal in the pipeline.

For each deal, ask: "What's the next action, and is it scheduled?"

| Stage | What to do if nothing is scheduled |
|-------|-------------------------------------|
| Qualified (no proposal sent) | Block time this week to write proposal |
| Proposal Sent | Has it been opened? Which day of the follow-up sequence are we on? |
| Proposal Opened | Call today if not done |
| Negotiating | Who moves it forward? What's blocking them? |
| Verbal Yes | Has the agreement been sent? If not, send today. |
| Contract Sent | Follow up daily until signed |
| Won (in onboarding) | 30-day or 90-day check-in coming up? |

**Red flag:** Any deal with no logged activity in 7+ days needs your attention today.

## DECISION RULES — WHEN TO CHANGE THE SYSTEM

**If cold email reply rate is <1% after 200+ sends:**
→ The offer isn't landing. Rewrite the first email. Test a different pain point.

**If discovery call → proposal rate is <40%:**
→ You're qualifying too loosely. Tighten the ICP. Not everyone on a discovery call should get a proposal.

**If proposal → close rate is <20% after 10+ proposals:**
→ Either the pricing is too high, the follow-up is stopping too early, or the proposal isn't connecting grant math to their specific numbers.

**If LinkedIn posts are getting <100 impressions per post after 4 weeks:**
→ Posting at wrong times, wrong content format, or connection count too low. Focus on growing connections before focusing on impressions.

**If no discovery calls after 30 days of outreach:**
→ Domain may be blacklisted (check Instantly.ai deliverability). Subject lines may be triggering spam filters. Try a different sending domain.
