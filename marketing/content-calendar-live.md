## ALGORITHM CHEAT SHEET

### TikTok
- Best times: 7pm–9pm local · Saturday 10am–12pm
- Hook must land in 1–2 seconds or you lose
- Use trending audio (muted or low) for 40% more reach
- 3–5 hashtags max (more = less reach)
- Respond to every comment in first hour (algorithm boost)

### Instagram Reels
- Best times: 9am, 12pm, 7pm weekdays
- Save rate > like rate = viral signal
- Add text overlays (65% watch without sound)
- 8–12 hashtags (mix sizes)
- Share to Stories immediately after posting

### LinkedIn
- Best times: Monday 8am, Tuesday–Wednesday 9am–11am
- Text-only posts can outperform video (algorithm favors native content)
- First comment within 5 minutes (post a question, reply to yourself)
- 3–5 hashtags max
- Tag relevant Michigan companies or thought leaders when appropriate

### YouTube Shorts
- Upload as "Short" (not regular video)
- Title must include keyword: "Michigan AI" / "AI automation" / "Michigan grants"
- 3 tags max
- Consistent posting = channel growth (YouTube rewards consistency)

### Facebook
- Best for: 35–65 age demo, grant content, healthcare and construction
- Best times: 1pm–3pm weekdays
- Boost grant content ($5/day) — highest ROI on Facebook
- Join and post in Michigan Business Facebook Groups
