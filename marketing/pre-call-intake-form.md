## HOW TO USE THE RESPONSES

**Before the call (10 minutes of prep):**

1. Read the Q3 answer (the specific process) and map it to your most relevant case study or ROI example
2. Note what tools they use (Q4) — look up whether you've integrated with them before
3. Check their revenue + employee count — it tells you which pricing tier makes sense
4. Identify 1–2 specific observations you can make on the call ("Based on what you told me, the likely bottleneck is X...")

**On the call:**
- Start by acknowledging what they wrote: "You mentioned [specific thing from Q3] — I want to start there because that's where most of the ROI lives for operations like yours."
- Use Q6 (their questions) as your agenda — you know what they need to hear
- Q5 (success definition) is gold — use it to anchor your close: "Based on what you told me, the definition of success is [X]. Here's how we'd get there..."

**After the call:**
- Log form responses in HubSpot contact record
- Note which answers matched the reality of the conversation vs. what they revealed live

## ALTERNATIVE: 3-QUESTION VERSION (for lower friction)

If the 6-question form has a low completion rate, switch to this shorter version:

**Email intro:**
> "Before we talk, one question that'll make the call more useful for you: What's the one process in your operation that costs the most time right now — and roughly how many hours per week does it take?"

Send as a direct email reply request. One question → higher response rate → still gets you the most important info.

## FORM SETUP INSTRUCTIONS

**Typeform (recommended for higher completion rates — cleaner UX):**
1. typeform.com → New Typeform → blank
2. Add questions in order above
3. Settings → Responses → "Send email notification" → your email
4. Share → get the link → save as "AAIS Strategy Call Intake" in bookmarks
5. Embed link in the email template above

**Google Forms (free, simpler):**
1. forms.google.com → Blank form
2. Add questions above
3. Settings → "Collect email addresses" → ON
4. Responses → link your Google Sheet for tracking
5. Share → "Shorten URL" → save the link

**HubSpot workflow automation:**
1. Create a HubSpot email template with the form link
2. Create a workflow: trigger = "Meeting booked" → action = "Send email [intake form email]"
3. Workflow fires automatically when a Calendly link is booked

## TRACKING COMPLETION

**Target:** 70%+ of booked prospects complete the form before the call

**If they don't complete it:**
- Don't cancel or reschedule — run the call, ask the questions live
- After the call, note which questions you needed to ask live that the form would have answered
- If low completion is a pattern, shorten the form or send it earlier (48 hours before, not 24)

**Completed form = higher close rate.** Prospects who fill out the form are more invested in the conversation before it starts. The form is a micro-commitment that increases show rates and close rates simultaneously.
