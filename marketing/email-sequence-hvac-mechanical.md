## WHY THIS VERTICAL

Michigan has thousands of HVAC, plumbing, and electrical contractors. The AI opportunity is massive and almost entirely untapped:

- **Dispatch optimization:** Service calls, tech routing, priority scheduling — almost all manual
- **Estimating acceleration:** Change orders and service estimates take 30–60 minutes each, manually
- **Preventive maintenance scheduling:** Customer base for recurring maintenance agreements
- **Parts/inventory intelligence:** Ordering patterns, seasonal demand forecasting
- **Service call documentation:** Techs document manually or skip it; AI can generate from voice notes

No national AI vendor focuses here. Michigan HVAC contractors don't go to tech conferences. This is a completely open market.
