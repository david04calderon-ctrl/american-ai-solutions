## QUICK PRODUCTION NOTES

- **Equipment:** iPhone 15 is enough. Clean background. Natural light from a window.
- **Detroit shots:** Brick walls, skyline views, warehouse aesthetic — all work.
- **No fancy editing:** Cut it, add captions (CapCut or DaVinci Resolve), export.
- **Captions always:** 85% of social video is watched without sound.
- **First 3 seconds:** Say the most interesting thing first. No intros. No "hey guys."
- **Face on camera:** Posts with your face consistently outperform screencaps alone.
  Alternate: face intro → demo screencap → face close.
