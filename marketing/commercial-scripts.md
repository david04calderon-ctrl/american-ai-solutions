## NOTES FOR PRODUCTION

When you're ready to shoot the 30-second spot:
- Film at a real Detroit business (ask a client or contact)
- David on camera for 5–10 seconds — direct, confident, no script feel
- B-roll: real workers, real operations, real Detroit
- No stock footage — Detroit businesses can tell
- Run it on: Hulu local (Michigan targeting), YouTube pre-roll, Facebook/Instagram geo-targeted to Detroit metro
- Local TV spot buy: WDIV, WXYZ, Fox 2 Detroit — local 30-second spots can run $200–$800 each
- Cable TV: Comcast Spotlight Detroit — targeted by zip code, can start at $500/week
