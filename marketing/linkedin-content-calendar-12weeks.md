# LinkedIn Content Calendar — 12 Weeks
# American AI Solutions LLC

**Goal:** 3 posts per week. One carousel (PDF), two text/image posts.
**When to post:** Tuesday and Thursday 8–10 AM EST perform best. Wednesday is solid. Avoid Monday morning and Friday.
**Why 3x/week:** Below 3 posts/week and the algorithm essentially ignores you. Above 5/week and quality drops. 3 is the sweet spot for a solo founder with actual work to do.
**Rule:** Every post ends with one of: a question (drives comments), a CTA to book a call, or a link to a blog post. Never just information — always a next step.

---

## POST TYPES

**Type A — Insight post (text-only or 1 image)**
Your opinion, observation, or lesson from working in Michigan business AI. 150–300 words max. Hook on line 1 is everything.

**Type B — Data post (text + numbers)**
A specific statistic, ROI breakdown, or before/after result. Concise. The number is the hook.

**Type C — Carousel (PDF)**
Use the carousel files in marketing/linkedin-carousel-01 through 04. Post one carousel per week.

**Type D — Story post**
A specific moment, conversation, or observation. First-person, specific, Michigan-relevant.

**Type E — Contrarian post**
Disagree with a common AI belief. Works well for engagement. Keep it respectful and factual.
