## AI VOICE SCRIPTS (ElevenLabs / Descript)

### Voiceover Direction for All AAIS Content:
```
Voice style: Confident, commanding, measured pace. Think: a trusted advisor who has seen it all and knows exactly what works. Not hype. Not sales pressure. Authority with warmth. Detroit energy — hardworking, real, no-nonsense. Emphasis on key words. Slight pause before the CTA.

Delivery notes:
- Slow down on key results ("TWENTY... hours a week... back.")
- Pause 0.5 seconds before the call to action
- Slightly lower, heavier on the brand name: "American. AI. Solutions."
- Upward inflection on questions, flat confidence on statements

Sample: "What does it cost your plant when a defect reaches the OEM? One recall event — fifty thousand dollars, minimum. We built a system that stopped that. In three weeks. Ninety-five percent defect detection. Zero defects shipped in sixty days. American AI Solutions. Link in bio."
```
