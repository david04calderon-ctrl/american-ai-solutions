### Day 29 — If No Client Yet — Reset

If you're at Day 29 with no signed client:
- **Don't panic.** B2B sales cycles for a $12K–$20K project typically run 30–60 days from first contact to signature. You likely have qualified prospects in the pipeline.
- **Audit:** Go through every HubSpot contact. For every conversation that didn't go anywhere, ask: Why? Too small? Wrong vertical? No urgency? Bad timing?
- **Adjust:** Based on the patterns — is it targeting (who you're reaching), messaging (what you're saying), or product (what you're offering)?

**What to do:**
- Book 5 more discovery calls this week, whatever it takes
- Re-engage any prospects who went quiet with the "three possible answers" email from proposal-follow-up-system.md
- Send founding client offer to 10 more targeted prospects

## THE DAILY MINIMUM

On any day where you have competing obligations and only 90 minutes for AAIS:

1. **Send 5 targeted direct messages or emails** — personalized, not mass. 15 minutes.
2. **Respond to every inbound message within 4 hours** — LinkedIn DMs, email replies, Calendly confirmations. 15 minutes.
3. **One LinkedIn activity** — post, comment on 5 relevant posts, or DM 5 new connections. 30 minutes.
4. **Update HubSpot** — log any conversations or activities from the day. 15 minutes.
5. **One building task** — demo progress, a new email draft, a case study outline, whatever is queued. 15 minutes.

90 minutes of focused execution per day compounds faster than 8 hours of unfocused activity.

---

## THE METRIC THAT MATTERS IN MONTH 1

**Discovery calls booked:** If you book 8–10 discovery calls in the first 30 days, you're on track. At a 25% close rate, that's 2 clients. At $12K average, that's $24K from Month 1 outreach — closing in Months 2–3.

**The leading indicator:** LinkedIn profile views + inbound connection requests trending up. If your profile views aren't growing week over week, your content isn't working. Fix the content before blaming the audience.

**The lagging indicator:** Signed clients. Don't optimize for signatures in Month 1 — optimize for conversations. Signatures follow conversations.
