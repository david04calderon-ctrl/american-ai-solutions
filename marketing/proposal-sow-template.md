### Pricing guidance by vertical:

| Vertical | Typical Project Range | Monthly Retainer Range |
|---|---|---|
| Automotive (Tier 2/3 suppliers) | $12,000–$35,000 | $1,500–$3,500/mo |
| Healthcare (private practices) | $8,000–$28,000 | $1,200–$2,800/mo |
| Logistics / Trucking | $10,000–$30,000 | $1,200–$2,500/mo |
| Construction / Trades | $6,000–$22,000 | $900–$2,000/mo |

**Never go below $5,000 for a project.** Under that, you're undervaluing the work and attracting price-sensitive clients who create support headaches.

**Always present 3 options:**
1. Core (smallest scope, lowest price)
2. Full (recommended — this proposal)
3. Full + Retainer (highest — but most value long-term)

The middle option is almost always chosen.
