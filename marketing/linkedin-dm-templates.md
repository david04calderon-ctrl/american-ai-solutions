# LinkedIn DM Templates — American AI Solutions LLC
**Use:** 15 outreach messages per day, Tuesday–Thursday (algorithm peak)
**Rule:** Never pitch in the first message. Open with a genuine question. Build rapport first.
**Goal:** Connection accepted → genuine conversation → call booked

---

## THE DM SEQUENCE (for all verticals)

### Message 1: Connection Request Note (300 characters max)
Sent with the connection request. Keep it short — this is the handshake.

### Message 2: Opener (sent 24–48 hours after connection accepted)
A single, genuine question. No pitch. No "I'd love to hop on a call."

### Message 3: Follow-up (sent 5–7 days if no response to Message 2)
Softer. Value-add. Not a second pitch.

### Message 4: Close (sent 5–7 days if still no response)
Short. Honest. Closes the loop. Leaves the door open.

## DAILY DM WORKFLOW (20 minutes/day)

### Morning (8–9am, M–Th):

1. **Search LinkedIn (5 min):**
   - Search for your target vertical + Michigan + relevant title
   - Filter: 2nd connections (warmer) first
   - Set a goal: 15 new connection requests per day

2. **Send Connection Requests (5 min):**
   - Scan their profile before sending (10 seconds)
   - Pick the most relevant connection note from the templates above
   - Personalize 1 detail if you see something obvious (recent job change, mutual connection, post they made)

3. **Check and Respond to Accepted Connections (5 min):**
   - Anyone who accepted in the last 24 hours → send the opener immediately
   - Personalize based on their title and what you saw on their profile

4. **Check and Respond to DM Replies (5 min):**
   - Every reply gets a response within 2 hours on weekdays
   - Don't let conversations go cold — momentum matters

### Weekly review (Friday, 5 min):
- How many connections sent? (Goal: 75/week)
- How many accepted? (Typical: 30–40%)
- How many openers sent? (= accepted connections from prior week)
- How many conversations started? (Goal: 3–5/week)
- How many calls booked? (Goal: 1–2/week after first month)
