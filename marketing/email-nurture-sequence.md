## HUBSPOT SETUP INSTRUCTIONS

**Create this as a Sequence (not a Workflow):**
- Sequences are for 1:1 personal emails — sends from your real Gmail
- This feels personal because it is personal
- Replies automatically pause the sequence (HubSpot handles this)

**Setup steps:**
1. HubSpot → Sequences → Create Sequence
2. Name it: "AIS New Lead — 14 Day Nurture"
3. Add 7 email steps with the delays above
4. Set sender: david@americanaisolutionsllc.com (connect Gmail in HubSpot settings)
5. Enroll contacts manually when a new lead comes in
   - Or set up a Workflow to auto-enroll: "Contact created → Enroll in AIS Nurture Sequence"

**Personalization tokens to add:**
- [First Name] — pulls from HubSpot contact first name
- [Company Name] — pull from company field if populated
- [Industry] — if you tag contacts by vertical, you can swap out industry examples

**Reply handling:**
- Any reply to any email removes them from the sequence automatically
- Reply goes to your Gmail — respond personally within 2 hours
- When they book: HubSpot shows the deal stage update → update to "Strategy Call Scheduled"

**After sequence ends (Day 14):**
- Move to "Long-term Newsletter" list
- Send 1 email per month: a Michigan case study, a grant update, or a new capability announcement
- These warm leads eventually convert — just on a longer timeline
