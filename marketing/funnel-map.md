## THE HYPNOTIC TOUCHPOINT SEQUENCE

A Michigan business owner typically needs **7–12 touchpoints** before booking.
Here's how we engineer all 12 without David doing extra work:

**Touchpoint 1:** LinkedIn post (organic reach)
**Touchpoint 2:** LinkedIn post (second view, different topic)
**Touchpoint 3:** Google search "Detroit AI consulting" → site appears
**Touchpoint 4:** Facebook retargeting ad (they visited the site)
**Touchpoint 5:** Instagram retargeting ad (same pixel)
**Touchpoint 6:** YouTube pre-roll before a business video they're watching
**Touchpoint 7:** TV spot while watching local news
**Touchpoint 8:** LinkedIn post again (different angle — grants, case study)
**Touchpoint 9:** Google search ad ("Michigan AI automation")
**Touchpoint 10:** They visit the site again — see new testimonial
**Touchpoint 11:** Email from HubSpot sequence (if they filled out any form)
**Touchpoint 12:** Call 1-888-AI-AGENT → Alex books them → David closes

## CONTENT LOOP — How One Win Becomes 10 Pieces of Content

When a client project ships:
1. **Case study** (1 Google Doc) → becomes:
2. **LinkedIn long-form post** (the story)
3. **LinkedIn short post** (one stat from it)
4. **Instagram carousel** (before/after, 5 slides)
5. **TikTok video** (30-second walkthrough)
6. **Email to HubSpot list** (new case study alert)
7. **Website testimonial** (add to index.html)
8. **Google Business post** (brief win summary)
9. **YouTube short** (clip from TikTok)
10. **Facebook ad** (boost the Instagram post)

One real client win = 10 pieces of content = 10 more touchpoints in the loop.
