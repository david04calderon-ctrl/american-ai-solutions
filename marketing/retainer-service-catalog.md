# Retainer Service Catalog
# American AI Solutions LLC

**Purpose:** Every completed project is a retainer opportunity. This catalog defines exactly what AAIS offers on a monthly retainer basis — what's included, what it costs, and how to present it. A retainer transforms a one-time $15K project into a $30K–$45K annual relationship. After Year 2, retainer revenue becomes the financial floor that makes client acquisition optional.
**When to introduce:** At the 90-day results call, after showing the ROI numbers. Never before.

---

## THE 3 RETAINER TIERS

### Tier 1 — System Maintenance & Monitoring
**Price:** $1,000–$1,500/month
**Best for:** Clients with a stable, well-trained system who need reliability assurance

**What's included:**
- Monthly system health check (performance, error rate, output accuracy review)
- Up to 5 hours of system adjustments per month (threshold changes, minor prompt updates, dashboard tweaks)
- Priority response to system issues: same day during business hours, next morning for off-hours
- Monthly 30-minute check-in call
- Quarterly metrics report (actual performance vs. targets from 90-day results call)
- Immediate notification if system output degrades below defined threshold

**What it's not:**
- New feature builds
- Additional integrations
- Major workflow changes
- Training additional staff (beyond 30-min onboarding for new hires)

**Pitch at 90-day call:**
> "The system is performing. The team knows how to use it. What I'd hate to see is something quietly drift — threshold needs a tweak, an API changes, the data structure shifts — and nobody notices until it's a problem. The Maintenance plan keeps someone watching it every month for $1,000. I check in, catch anything small before it's big, and you have same-day response if something breaks. Want me to include that in our Year 2 conversation?"

---

### Tier 2 — Active Optimization
**Price:** $2,000–$2,500/month
**Best for:** Clients who want continuous improvement, not just stability

**What's included — everything in Tier 1, plus:**
- Up to 10 hours of development per month (new prompts, new views, expanded integrations)
- Monthly "improvement session" — 60-min call to review what's working, what to optimize next
- 1 minor new feature or integration per quarter (under 5 hours build time)
- KPI trending dashboard — live view of month-over-month performance metrics
- Proactive recommendations: David flags opportunities based on new AI capabilities or changes in your industry
- Access to new AI tools evaluated and tested before they're relevant to your operation

**Example quarterly additions included at Tier 2:**
- Add a new dashboard panel when you request one
- Adjust AI output format for a new OEM requirement
- Add a new email recipient to a report distribution list
- Tune alert thresholds as operations change
- Integrate a new data source that becomes available

**Pitch at 90-day call:**
> "The $2,000/month tier is for clients who want the system to keep getting better, not just stay the same. Every month we spend 10 hours actively improving — based on what your team is experiencing and where AI is headed. You never have to wonder if you're falling behind. I'll tell you what's new, what applies to you, and we build it."

---

### Tier 3 — Strategic AI Partner
**Price:** $3,500–$4,500/month
**Best for:** Clients who want AAIS as their dedicated AI department — ongoing builds + strategic guidance

**What's included — everything in Tier 2, plus:**
- Up to 20 hours of development per month
- 1 major new module per quarter (full feature build, separate from hours)
- Bi-weekly strategy call (30 min) — what's coming, what to prioritize, what to avoid
- AI vendor evaluation — when a new tool enters the market, David evaluates and recommends before you're sold by a vendor
- Staff training on new capabilities as they're added
- Annual AI roadmap document — 12-month plan for AI investment across the operation
- Priority access: if David is at capacity, Tier 3 clients get scheduled first
- Board/leadership presentation available (1x per year): translate the AI program into executive language

**Pitch at 90-day call:**
> "Some clients want to move fast. At $3,500/month, you essentially have a part-time AI director — someone who knows your systems, knows where AI is going, and makes sure you're always 12 months ahead of your competitors. That means new builds every quarter, strategy calls every two weeks, and someone whose job is to make sure your AI investment keeps compounding."

## THE RETAINER CONVERSION CONVERSATION

**Setting:** 90-day results call, after presenting the ROI numbers.

**Script:**

> "So the numbers look like: [review results]. That's [dollar ROI] back per year from a $[project cost] investment. That's a [X:1] return.
>
> [Pause]
>
> Here's what I want to bring up. Right now, the system is working. But AI doesn't stand still — the tools improve, your data changes, your team grows. The clients who keep seeing compounding returns are the ones who stay connected to the technology.
>
> I have a maintenance and optimization program. At the basic level, it's $1,000 a month — I watch the system, catch issues before you see them, make small adjustments monthly. At the next level, $2,000, we're actively improving every month — new features, new integrations, staying ahead of what your OEM or payer is going to require next.
>
> It's not a requirement. The system runs without it. But clients who stay on retainer get 2–3x the value from the original investment over two years, because we keep building.
>
> Which of those levels sounds right for where you are?"

**Then stop talking.** Let them respond. Most say Tier 1 or Tier 2 immediately if the 90-day results are strong.

## RETAINER PRICING RATIONALE

### The "how did you arrive at this price?" question

> "The pricing reflects the value of having a dedicated expert watching and improving your system every month. An in-house AI engineer costs $90,000–$130,000 annually — that's $7,500–$11,000 per month, plus benefits, plus management overhead, plus the ramp time to understand your specific systems.
>
> The Tier 2 retainer at $2,000/month gives you all of the expertise with none of the overhead. And because I built your system, there's zero ramp time — I already know exactly how it works."

### The ROI framing for retainers

At Tier 2 ($2,000/month = $24,000/year):
- Year 1 project: $15,000 → System generates $60,000+ annual savings
- Retainer keeps that ROI compounding: adds 2–3 new improvements per year
- Each improvement = additional savings layer
- Net ROI of retainer: typically 3–5x retainer cost per year

Show this math explicitly on the 90-day call. Clients who see it in numbers say yes faster.

## RETAINER CHURN PREVENTION

**Warning signs a retainer is at risk:**
- Missed 2 consecutive monthly check-in calls
- Client team turnover (champion who signed leaves)
- Invoice paid late (first time)
- Response times to your messages slowing down
- "Everything's fine" answers on check-in calls with no new questions

**When you see warning signs — act immediately:**

> "[Name] — I want to be direct. I've noticed [specific signal]. I want to make sure the retainer is still delivering what you need. Can we carve out 20 minutes this week to talk about whether the current structure still fits? I'd rather adjust and keep the relationship than have you feel like it's not worth it."

Most at-risk retainers can be saved with one proactive conversation. The ones that churn without conversation are the ones where the problem was visible 60 days earlier and ignored.
