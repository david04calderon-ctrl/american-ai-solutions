## THE 3 CORE TALK FRAMEWORKS

### Talk 1 — Automotive/Manufacturing
**Title:** "OEM AI Mandate to ROI: What Michigan Tier 2 Suppliers Need to Know in 2026"
**Duration:** 20–45 minutes (scalable)
**Audience:** Plant managers, quality directors, operations VPs, supply chain leads

**Outline:**
1. (3 min) The mandate is real — what OEMs are actually requiring and when
2. (5 min) The gap: what Michigan Tier 2/3 suppliers currently have vs. what OEMs will expect
3. (10 min) 3 real implementations: quality dashboard, defect detection, supplier scorecard [anonymized]
4. (5 min) The grant math: Going PRO + Industry 4.0 = most Michigan manufacturers can implement for under $5K net
5. (5 min) Q&A
6. (2 min) Close: "If this applies to your operation, I do a free 30-minute strategy call — no pitch, just the real conversation."

**Key data points:**
- OEM defect detection threshold: many now requiring 95%+ accuracy
- Average Michigan Tier 2 quality reporting: 18–25 hrs/week (manual)
- After AI automation: 4–6 hrs/week
- Going PRO reimbursement: up to $50K/year per employer

### Talk 2 — All Verticals (Chamber / SBDC / Mixed Audience)
**Title:** "5 AI Wins for Michigan Small Businesses — Real Numbers, Real Operations"
**Duration:** 25–40 minutes
**Audience:** Mixed Michigan business owners, any vertical

**Outline:**
1. (3 min) Who this is for: businesses with repetitive processes eating time and money
2. (3 min) What AI actually is vs. what vendors claim it is
3. (15 min) 5 Michigan case studies (one per major vertical + ROI):
   - Manufacturing: quality reporting automation (22 hrs → 6 hrs/week)
   - Healthcare: prior auth AI (28 hrs → 6 hrs/week, 11% → 4% denial rate)
   - Logistics: dispatch optimization (22% → 16% deadhead = $108K/yr recovered)
   - Construction: estimating AI (14 hrs → 3 hrs/bid, 5 → 9 bids/month)
   - Cross-vertical: AI-assisted customer communication (response time from hours to minutes)
4. (5 min) The grant angle — Michigan funding that covers 50% of implementation
5. (5 min) The honest "when AI doesn't work" — setting expectations
6. (5 min) Q&A + close

### Talk 3 — Healthcare
**Title:** "HIPAA + AI: What Michigan Healthcare Practices Need to Know Before They Buy"
**Duration:** 30–45 minutes
**Audience:** Practice managers, health system operations, clinical directors

**Outline:**
1. (5 min) The AI hype in healthcare vs. what's actually HIPAA-compliant
2. (5 min) The BAA framework: which vendors have it, which don't, what it means for you
3. (10 min) 3 HIPAA-safe AI use cases Michigan practices are implementing now:
   - Prior authorization automation
   - Patient communication AI (scheduling, reminders, follow-up)
   - Billing denial reduction
4. (5 min) On-premise vs. cloud: when each is right
5. (5 min) The "audit proof" standard — what documentation looks like for HIPAA AI compliance
6. (5 min) Q&A + close
