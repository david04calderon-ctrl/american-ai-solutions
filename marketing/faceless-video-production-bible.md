## METHOD 2 — ELEVENLABS + CAPCUT (Most Professional Output)

This is your quality play. Sounds like a real professional presenter. Use for LinkedIn and YouTube.

### Step 1: Set Up Your AI Voice

**Option A — Clone David's voice (BEST):**
1. Go to **elevenlabs.io** → Voices → Add Voice → Instant Voice Clone
2. Record a 2-minute voice memo on your phone: read the brand statement slowly and clearly
3. Upload → name it "David Calderon — AAIS"
4. Now every video sounds like YOU, forever

**Option B — Use "Adam" voice (Start immediately):**
1. Go to elevenlabs.io → Text to Speech
2. Select "Adam" voice
3. Settings: Stability 55%, Similarity 75%, Style 40%
4. This voice is deep, authoritative, American — fits AAIS perfectly

### Step 2: Generate Voiceover

Paste any script from `marketing/faceless-content-scripts.md` → Generate → Download MP3

**Voiceover formatting tips for ElevenLabs:**
- Add `...` for a half-second pause
- Add `[pause]` before the CTA
- ALL CAPS on key stats: "TWENTY hours a week — back in your operation."
- Use periods at the end of every line to force natural emphasis

### Step 3: Build in CapCut

1. New Project → 9:16 vertical (TikTok/Reels) or 16:9 (LinkedIn/YouTube)
2. Add dark background: black rectangle or `#0D1117` background image
3. Import your MP3 voiceover → lay it on the timeline
4. Add free stock video from **Pexels.com** — search:
   - "manufacturing plant" → Michigan/Detroit B-roll feel
   - "business meeting" → professional environment
   - "data dashboard" → AI/tech visual
   - "trucking highway" → logistics content
   - "construction site" → trades content
5. Add text overlays:
   - Font: Bold sans-serif (closest to DM Sans: Montserrat Bold in CapCut)
   - Color: White text, `#00D4FF` cyan for key stats
   - Size: Large — readable without sound
6. Add AAIS logo (use `public/logo-white.svg`) → top-left corner, small
7. End card: "American AI Solutions LLC · americanaisolutionsllc.com"
8. Add music: CapCut library → "Corporate" or "Tech" → volume 15% (under voice)
9. Export: 1080p for all platforms

## METHOD 3 — CANVA TEXT-ON-SCREEN REELS (No Voice Needed)

**Best for:** Instagram Reels, TikTok, LinkedIn short-form
**Why it works:** 65% of social videos are watched without sound. Text-on-screen content gets MORE engagement on LinkedIn.

### The Template:

1. Open Canva → New Design → 1080 × 1920 (Reel/Story)
2. Set background to `#0D1117` (Deep Navy)
3. Add a thin cyan horizontal bar at the top: 3px line, color `#00D4FF`
4. Use this slide structure:

**Slide 1 (0-3s) — THE HOOK:**
```
[LARGE WHITE TEXT — 72px DM Serif Display or Playfair Display]
"[HOOK LINE]"

[SMALL CYAN TEXT below — 18px uppercase]
AMERICAN AI SOLUTIONS
```

**Slide 2 (3-15s) — THE BODY (one point per second):**
```
[Each bullet appears with a fade animation]
• [POINT 1]
• [POINT 2]  
• [POINT 3]

[Bottom: thin cyan accent bar]
```

**Slide 3 (15-20s) — THE CTA:**
```
[Bold white text]
→ Book a Free Strategy Call

[Cyan text below]
americanaisolutionsllc.com

[Logo bottom-right]
```

5. Download as MP4 with animations
6. In CapCut: add trending audio (muted or 10% volume) → this triggers TikTok/Instagram algorithm
7. Post

## THE REFRAME — WHY NO PHOTOGRAPHY IS FINE RIGHT NOW

The biggest mistake founders make: waiting until the brand is "perfect" to start posting.

The truth: **faceless content about your results outperforms founder content 80% of the time on TikTok and Instagram.** People want to know what you can do for them. Not what you look like.

David's face and voice are the GROWTH ACCELERANT when you add them.
But results-based faceless content is what BUILDS THE AUDIENCE to receive it.

Start posting now.
Add your face when the photography is ready.
The audience you build in the meantime is the one that's ready when you arrive.
