## SLIDE 8 — HOW TO APPLY (SIMPLIFIED)

**Visual:** 5-step numbered process — clean, readable

**Headline:** `How to access these grants:`

**Steps:**
```
1. Find your Michigan Works! agency
   → michiganworks.org → "Find Your Agency"

2. Call or email — mention Going PRO
   → Tell them you're implementing an
     AI system and training your team

3. Complete the pre-application form
   → They'll ask about: # of employees,
     training description, employer match

4. Submit with training plan docs
   → Your AI vendor helps with this part

5. File reimbursement after training
   → With documented attendance logs
     and competency assessments
```

**Footer:** `Total time: 2–4 hours of paperwork. Potential return: $10,000–$50,000.`
