## SLIDE 4 — PRIOR AUTHORIZATION AUTOMATION

**Headline:** Prior auth was designed to slow you down. We automate it.

**How it works:**

1. AI reads the incoming referral/order
2. Pulls the patient's existing records and diagnosis codes automatically
3. Generates the prior auth request in the payer's required format
4. Submits via the payer portal or fax API
5. Monitors for approval/denial and flags exceptions for staff review
6. Approved in the same day in 80%+ of cases vs. 3–5 days manually

**Time saved:** 15–20 hours/week at a typical 5-provider practice

**Cost of delay removed:** Prior auth denials cost Michigan practices an average of $14.50 per denied claim in administrative follow-up time.

## SLIDE 5 — SCHEDULING & NO-SHOW REDUCTION

**Headline:** Every empty slot is money that's already been lost.

**The AI scheduling layer we deploy:**

- Automated appointment reminders (SMS + email) at 48hr and 2hr marks
- Intelligent rebooking: when a cancellation comes in, AI immediately offers the slot to the next person on the waitlist
- No-show prediction: flags high-risk appointments for a personal call from staff
- Post-visit follow-up sequences: automated check-ins at 3, 7, and 30 days (for chronic conditions)

**Result at comparable Michigan practices:**
- No-show rate: 21% → 11% (near-industry benchmark)
- Slot utilization: 74% → 89%
- Patient satisfaction scores: +12 points on average
