## POSTING RULES

1. **Always short paragraphs** — never more than 2 lines per paragraph
2. **White space is content** — blank lines between every thought
3. **No hashtag spam** — max 3 hashtags, at the bottom, never in the copy
4. **Hook in line 1** — if it doesn't make them stop scrolling, rewrite it
5. **One CTA per post** — don't give them 3 options
6. **Post at 7–9am EST** — Michigan business owners are up early
7. **Reply to every comment within 2 hours** — the algorithm rewards it
8. **Never delete a post** — even low-performers teach you something
