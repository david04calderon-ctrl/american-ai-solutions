Cold Call Openers
🏭 Auto Supplier Cold Call
Proven
When they pick up:
"Hey [Name], this is David with American AI Solutions out of Detroit. I'll be super quick — we work with Tier 2 and Tier 3 suppliers in Michigan helping them hit OEM AI readiness standards without spending a fortune. Do you have 90 seconds for me to tell you what that looks like?"
If they say "we're not interested":
"Totally respect that. Can I ask — is that because you've already got an AI plan, or because you haven't had time to look into it yet? I ask because most of the suppliers I talk to who say that end up being the ones who need it most before the next OEM audit."
🏥 Healthcare Opener
"Hi, is this [Name]? This is David from American AI Solutions — we help Michigan medical practices cut admin hours in half using HIPAA-compliant AI. I know your time is incredibly valuable, so I'll ask one question: how many hours a week is your team spending on prior auth and paperwork that isn't direct patient care?"
Objection Handlers
"It's too expensive."
"I hear that a lot before people see the math. Our average client saves $3,000 to $5,000 a month in labor — so the system pays for itself in 3 to 5 months. And Michigan's Going PRO grant can cut the upfront cost in half. Can I show you the numbers for your specific situation?"
"We tried AI before and it didn't work."
"Yeah — most of the 'AI' tools out there are just ChatGPT wrappers that don't connect to anything real. What we build is custom-integrated into your actual workflow — your tools, your data, your team. It's night and day different. What did you try before, out of curiosity?"
"I need to think about it."
"Completely fair. What would help you make a decision? Because usually 'I need to think about it' means one of two things — either the value isn't totally clear yet, or the timing isn't right. Which one is it for you?"
"We don't have the technical staff for this."
"Good news — you don't need any. We build it, we deploy it, we train your team, and we stay with you 30 days post-launch to make sure everything runs. Your team clicks buttons. We handle the engineering."
Closing Lines
✅
"Based on everything you've told me, the numbers make sense — and we have the bandwidth to start in the next two weeks. What would it take to get a yes today?"
✅
"I'm going to send over the proposal tonight. Fair warning — I only work with 3 new clients a month. Once those spots are gone, the next open slot is 8 weeks out."
✅
"The grant deadline is [date]. If we start the application now, we can get you 50% covered. If we wait, you pay full price. Your call."
