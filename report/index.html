David Calderon and the AAIS team review every intake form personally. This is not an automated PDF generator. Your intake answers are analyzed against Michigan market data, OEM audit requirements, grant eligibility criteria, and vertical-specific automation benchmarks. It takes about 4–6 hours to produce.

The core AI analysis works for any US business. The Michigan grant section will simply note that state-specific programs may differ. We can still identify federal-level funding sources and general industry grants. Most of our clients are Michigan-based, but we build AI systems nationwide.

Yes, 100%. If you read your report and honestly feel it wasn't useful or didn't apply to your business, email us and we'll refund the full $197. No questions, no hassle. We'd rather refund a dissatisfied client than keep their money and lose their trust.

We do offer a 15-minute walkthrough call to explain the findings, but it's optional and free. We're not going to use a $197 report as a Trojan horse for a pitch.
