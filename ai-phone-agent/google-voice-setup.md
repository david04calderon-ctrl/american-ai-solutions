### TIPS FOR RECORDING

- Quiet room. No background noise.
- Take a breath before you hit record.
- Speak like you're talking to ONE person, not reading.
- Say "We" — sounds like a team.
- Record it 2–3 times, pick the best take.
- Listen back before saving.

## STEP 3 — SET UP GOOGLE VOICE (5 minutes)

In Google Voice settings, configure:

**Incoming calls:**
- Forward to: [your cell phone number]
- Do not disturb: OFF (you want every call coming through)
- Ring for: 25 seconds before voicemail

**Voicemail notifications:**
- Email notification: ON (goes to david04calderon@gmail.com)
- Transcription: ON (Google transcribes the voicemail so you can read it instantly)
- SMS notification to your cell: ON

**Hours (when to forward vs. go straight to voicemail):**
- Business hours (Mon–Fri 9am–6pm EST): Forward to cell
- After hours: Straight to voicemail
- This way callers get you live when you're available, and a professional voicemail after hours
