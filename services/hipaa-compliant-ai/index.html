Why "ChatGPT for healthcare" isn't HIPAA-compliant — and what is

Public AI models (ChatGPT, Claude via consumer API, Google Gemini) do not offer a BAA for standard subscriptions. Entering PHI into these systems is a HIPAA violation — regardless of whether you intend to use the output for clinical purposes.


The systems we build use Claude API via Anthropic's enterprise agreements (which include BAA), on-premise models (Llama 3.1 / Mistral on your own hardware), or Microsoft Azure OpenAI — all with proper BAA execution. Your data stays in a compliant environment from intake to output.


Every Michigan practice we work with receives a completed BAA before we begin any scoping conversation involving patient data.
